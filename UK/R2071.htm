Orange Sponge Cake

   FOR 12 UNITS

100 g of flour.

150 g of sugar.

1 orange.

4 eggs.

Butter for the mold.

Preheat the oven. Grease the mold with butter and place parchment paper, also greased, on the bottom. Beat the egg whites till stiff and mix with the yolks and the sugar. Sift the flour. Squeeze the orange and grate the peel. Mix the flour, the grated peel and the juice with the eggs. Place in the mold. Bake for 25 minutes at a moderate temperature. Cut the sponge cake in the mold and remove when cool.
