   FOR 6 SERVINGS

1 kg of white fish, a common dentex, for example

1 kg of tomatoes

100 g of almonds

6 cloves of garlic

1/2 l oil

Salt

Pepper

Roast the tomatoes in the oven, until almost completely reduced. Meanwhile, heat the oil and fry the garlic and the almonds. Set aside. If the fish is large, cut into round slices, sprinkle with salt and fry on both sides. Soak up the excess oil on absorbent paper and place in a casserole dish or pan. Puree the tomatoes, together with the crushed garlic and almonds. Pour the sauce over the fish, adding the necessary amount of salt and pepper. Simmer lightly for 2 or 3 minutes.
