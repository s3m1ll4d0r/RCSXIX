Citron or Lemon Rind Marmalade

   FOR APPROXIMATELY 800 cc

1 kg of boiled citron lemon peel.

1.2 kg of sugar.

Salt.

Citron lemons are those which have a rough and very thick peel, and are ideal for this purpose. Cut the peel into strips, place in water with a little salt added and leave for 24 hours. Rinse and place in a saucepan, cover with water and boil for 2 or 3 minutes. Discard the water and pour hot water over the peels. When the water cools, strain and add more hot water. Repeat 4 or 5 times, until the peel looses its bitterness. Strain and weigh. Use 1.2 kg. of sugar for every kilogram of peel. With the sugar and 500 cc of water make a syrup to a thread consistency. Add the peel and boil on a low flame while stirring often, until the peel is nearly translucent and the marmalade thick.
