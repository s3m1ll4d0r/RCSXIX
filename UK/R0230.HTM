   FOR 6 SERVINGS

1 kg of tomatoes

100 g of rice

100 cc of oil

2 beef bouillon cubes

Wash the tomatoes and cut them in half. Cook them with the beef cubes and a litre of water. When they are well done strain them. Add the rice and simmer at a low flame until the rice is tender.

Per Serving

Fats (G) 17 Fibre (G) 1
