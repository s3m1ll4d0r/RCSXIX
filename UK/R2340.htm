   FOR 6 SERVINGS

300 g of sugar

225 g of ground almonds

6 egg yolks

3 egg whites

Grated lemon peel

Cinnamon powder

Butter for the mold

Make a syrup using the sugar and 250 cc of water. Add the grated lemon peel and the cinnamon. Let cool. Grease the mold with butter. Beat the egg yolks until they are frothy and mix with the almonds. Mix with the syrup. Beat the egg whites till stiff and combine with the previous mixture using a wooden spoon, being careful to not let it fall. Pour the mix into the mold and place it in the oven, preheated to a medium temperature, for 35 minutes.
