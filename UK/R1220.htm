   FOR 4 SERVINGS

200 g of rice

500 g of pork

4 bananas

125 cc of oil

6 cloves of garlic

Salt

Cumin

Paprika

Parsley

Crush the garlic together with the paprika, salt, cumin and parsley. Add 2 tablespoons of oil. Cut the meat into pieces and let marinate for at least 2 hours. Cook the white rice. Cut the bananas in 2 or 3 pieces and fry in hot oil. Place on absorbent paper. Fry the meat in the oil that is left over. Place the bananas on a serving tray. Serve the remaining sauce with the rice.
