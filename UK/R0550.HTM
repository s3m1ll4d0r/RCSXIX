   FOR 8 SERVINGS

1/2 kg of cabbage

1 head of lettuce

150 g of "serrano" ham (a type of cured ham)

1 chicken breast

1 onion

1 tomato

12 slices of toasted bread

1 1/2 l of broth

Cook the cabbage, the lettuce, the chicken and the ham in the broth, all whole. Chop the onion and the tomato and add to the soup. When the cabbage is tender take it out of the broth together with the lettuce, the chicken and the ham. Finely chop the ingredients and put them into the bowls in which the slices of bread have been placed. When it is time to serve pour the hot broth on top.
