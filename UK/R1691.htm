   FOR 300 cc

1 tablespoon of almonds

1 tablespoon of raisins

125 cc of white wine

125 cc of oil

2 tablespoons of vinegar

1 lemon

Parsley

Saffron

Salt

Mix all the ingredients together with a beater, except the raisins, which should be added afterwards.
