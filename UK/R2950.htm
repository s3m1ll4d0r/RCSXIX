Coconut Marmalade

   FOR APPROXIMATELY 800 cc

1/2 kg of grated coconut.

1/2 kg sugar.

4 egg yolks.

On a low flame, dissolve the sugar in 250 cc of water. Without waiting to allow it to thicken, add the coconut. Cook on a low flame while stirring constantly. When the coconut is done and the mixture starts to thicken, add the beaten yolks. Continue stirring until the yolks are done.
