Potatoes with Bacon

   FOR 6 SERVINGS

1 kg of potatoes

200 g of lean bacon

100 g of oil

50 g of flour

Salt

Pepper

Parsley

Cook the flour in the oil. Add the lean bacon, cut into small pieces, and the salt, pepper and parsley. When the bacon begins to brown, cut the potatoes into round slices. Add 375 cc of water. Cover and cook at a low flame, stirring occasionally.
