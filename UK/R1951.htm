Apple Pudding

   FOR 8 SERVINGS

1 kg of pippin apples.

150 g of sugar.

250 g of sponge fingers

100 g of ground almonds.

5 eggs.

Grated Lemon peel.

Butter for the mold.

Peel the apples, remove the core and cook in 2 tablespoons of water till tender. Drain and let cool. Crumble the sponge fingers and the apples and mix with the almonds, the sugar and the lemon peel. Beat the eggs, yolks and whites together, and add to the previous mixture. Heat the oven, grease the mold with the butter and place the mixture in the mold. Double boil in the oven for 45 minutes. Remove from the mold when cool.
