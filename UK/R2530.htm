Potato Pie

   FOR 6 SERVINGS

150 g of black potatoes (A popular Canarian variety)

150 g of almonds

300 g of sugar

4 eggs

Cinnamon

Grated lemon peel

Dough:

150 g of flour

1 egg yolk

60 g of butter

Glaze:

200 g of confectioners' sugar

1 lemon

1 egg white

Boil the potatoes in their skins. Peel and break them up. Mix with the ground almonds and the sugar. Add the cinnamon powder and the grated lemon peel. Mix well and slowly add the whole eggs, one by one, mixing with a wooden spoon. Grease the mold with the butter. Preheat the oven. Make the dough using the butter, the flour and the egg yolk. Flatten out very thin with a rolling pin. Line the bottom and the sides and cover, saving a piece for the top. Place the filling inside, cover with the piece of dough you saved and join the edges securely. Bake in the oven at a medium temperature for 45 minutes. Meanwhile, use the confectioners' sugar and the lemon juice to prepare the glaze and add a lightly beaten egg white, slowly with a teaspoon. After taking the pie out of the oven, cover with the glaze and decorate with colored sprinkles.
