   FOR 450 g

1 l of cream

Salt

Beat the cream, preferably with an electric beater, until the whey and the fat separate. Gather all the fat with your hands, after first wetting them with cold water, and drain off the excess whey. Wash several times with water, as cold as possible, until the fat is totally transparent. Salt to taste.
