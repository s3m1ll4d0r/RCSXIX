Potato Soup with Toast

Index

   FOR 4 SERVINGS

4 slices of toasted bread

250 g of potatoes

2 egg yolks

1 lemon

1 teaspoon of sugar

1 l of broth

Parsley

Cut the potatoes in round slices and cook in the stock with the minced parsley. Put the toasted bread slices in a heat- resistant soup bowl, placing the cooked potatoes and the stock on top. Beat the egg yolks with the lemon juice and sugar added. Pour the yolks over the top of the broth, little by little, to keep them from falling to the bottom. Place the soup in the oven and leave for 5 minutes, or until the top is toasted.
