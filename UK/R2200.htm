Sweet Orange Caramel Custard

   FOR 6 SERVINGS

2 oranges

250 g of sugar.

1 egg.

6 egg yolks.

Cut the oranges into very fine slices. Mix with 200 g of sugar. Cover and let stand for 8 hours. Make a caramel in the mold using the remaining 50 g of sugar and a tablespoon of water. Beat the oranges and the sugar and strain. Lightly beat the yolks with the whole egg. Mix with the orange purée. Place in the mold and cover. Double boil for one hour. Remove from the mold when it cools.
