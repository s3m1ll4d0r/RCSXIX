Moray Eel

   FOR 4 SERVINGS

1 kg of moray eel (in round slices)

1 red pepper

250 cc of oil

100 cc of vinegar

250 cc of fish stock (you can use cubes)

Bread without the crust

Heat the oil and fry the red pepper cut in strips. Remove and, in the same oil, fry the moray eel slices, which have been previously washed well and dried. When they are done on both sides place them in a casserole dish or pan. Beat the bread with the vinegar and the fish stock. Pour this over the moray and add the strips of red pepper. Simmer for 4 or 5 minutes.
