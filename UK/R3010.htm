   FOR 1500 cc (APPROX.)

2 l of must

2 kg of different fruits (apples, pears, plums, apricots, etc...)

400 g of sugar

Mix the must with the sugar and boil until it is reduced by half. Cut the fruit into large pieces, with the peel, and remove the pits. Cover with water and boil for a few minutes. Drain and add to the reduced must. Simmer while stirring frequently with a wooden spoon until the fruit forms a very thick syrup.
