   FOR 15 UNITS

200 g of sugar.

50 g of confectioners' sugar.

3 egg whites.

Lemon peel.

Use the sugar, 125 cc of water and the lemon peel to make a syrup to a very thick thread consistency. Remove the lemon peel. Beat the whites till very stiff, while adding half the confectioners' sugar. Pour the syrup over the whites while beating, taking care that the syrup is not poured directly onto the whisk, for it would cristalize. Let the mixture settle. Meanwhile, preheat the oven to a very moderate temperature. Prepare the baking pans covering them with parchment paper. Prepare the meringues, either using a pastry tube or a spoon, making sure they don't touch. Place in the oven and bake for one and a half hours or until they start to form a crust. When removed from the oven, they can be joined in pairs and sprinkled with the rest of the sugar.
