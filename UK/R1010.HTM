Cod Casserole

Index

   FOR 4 SERVINGS

400 g of desalted cod

2 slices of toasted bread

1 tablespoon of chopped parsley

250 cc of oil

125 cc of white wine

9-10 cloves of garlic

Toasted saffron

Cover the cod with water and bring it to a boil. When it starts boiling, remove from the flame and drain, keeping the water for later. Remove the skin and bones. Mix the rest of the ingredients with a beater along with 500 cc of the water used to boil the fish. Put a little sauce in the bottom of a earthenware casserole dish and alternate with one layer of fish another layer of sauce until all the ingredients are used. Cook at a low flame for 15-20 minutes.
