   FOR 8 SERVINGS

150 g of ground almonds.

12 sponge fingers (see simple sponge fingers which ...)

4 eggs.

250 g of sugar.

150 cc of orange liqueur

Cinnamon.

Grated orange peel.

Butter for the mold.

Crumble the sponge fingers and mix with the almonds, the sugar, the cinnamon, the orange peel, the liqueur and the egg yolks. Heat the oven. Grease the mold with the butter. Beat the egg whites till stiff and mix with the almond paste. Place in the mold and double boil in the oven for 50 minutes at a medium temperature.
