   FOR 25 UNITS

200 g of flour.

100 g of lard.

100 g of sugar.

1 egg yolk.

1 tablespoon of rose water.

Preheat the oven. Prepare the baking pans by covering them with tin foil which has been slightly dampened with cold water. Beat the lard with the sugar and the yolk. Add the rose water and mix everything with the flour. Shape the cookies with your hands, making them about 1 cm. high. Place on the pans and bake for 20 minutes at a medium temperature.
