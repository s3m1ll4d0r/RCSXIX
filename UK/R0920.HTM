Marinade for Sea Bream or other Fish

   FOR 4 SERVINGS

1 kg of sea bream or other fish

Black pepper

Salt

4 oranges

4 lemons

1/2 l of oil

Clove

Saffron

Wash the fish, cut into pieces and sprinkle with salt and pepper. Squeeze the juice from the oranges and the lemons. Mix the juice with the crushed clove and saffron. Heat the oil and fry the fish. Put the pieces of fish in the juice. Filter the oil and pour over the marinade.
