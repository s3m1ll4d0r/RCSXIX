   FOR 4 SERVINGS

250 g of ground beef

2 eggs

100 g of chopped almonds

1 onion

4 cloves of garlic

100 cc of oil

Chopped nutmeg

Crushed clove

Salt

Chop the onion and the garlic very fine and fry in the hot oil. When the onions are transparent add the almonds. Let cool a little and then mix with the meat, together with the eggs, lightly beaten, and the spices. Fry everything in a non-stick frying pan, turning constantly. This dish goes very well with white rice, eggs and fried bananas.
