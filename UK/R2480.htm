Lemon Caramel Custard

   FOR 8 SERVINGS

400 g of sugar

20 egg yolks

10 lemons

100 g of sugar, for the caramel

Beat the eggs with the sugar. Wash the lemons with hot water and cut them in thin round slices. Mix with the beaten eggs and let stand for one hour. Strain the mixture. Make the caramel in the mold with 100 g of sugar and 2 tablespoons of water. Put the egg mixture in the mold, cover and double boil for 50 minutes.
