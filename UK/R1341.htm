   FOR 4 SERVINGS

200 g of cooked ground beef

1 onion

2 cloves of garlic

2 eggs

20 g of bread

4 tablespoons of milk

1/2 l of oil (for frying)

Parsley

Salt

Chop the onion very fine and fry in 2 tablespoons of oil. Meanwhile, soak the bread with the milk and let it dissolve. Mix the meat with the eggs, the bread, the fried onion, the parsley and the garlic crushed with the salt. Beat the egg whites lightly, with a little salt. Make the cakes and dip them in the egg whites. Fry in very hot oil.
