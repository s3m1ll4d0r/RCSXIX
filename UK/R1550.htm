Meat and Egg Casserole

Index

   FOR 6 SERVINGS

500 g of ground beef

500 g of ground pork

100 g of lean bacon

4 eggs

100 g of sugar

100 g of pine nuts

2 cooked red peppers

1 onion

125 cc of oil

125 cc of white wine

Parsley

Clove

Cinnamon

Nutmeg

Salt

Chop the onion and fry it in the oil with the chopped parsley. When the onion is translucent add the bacon, cut into small cubes. Add the meat and the wine and cook at a low flame, adding the ground spices, the salt and the pine nuts. Peel the red peppers, cut them in strips and set aside. Beat the eggs with the sugar until frothy and add 125 cc of water. Place the meat in a baking pan, alternating with the red pepper strips. Heat the eggs and water over a flame and continue beating until thick. Pour over the meat and place in a very hot oven to bake for 5 minutes or until brown.
