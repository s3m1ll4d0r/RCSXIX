"Tocino de Cielo" (egg and syrup sweet).

   FOR 8 UNITS

10 egg yolks.

1 egg white.

250 g of sugar.

Make a syrup to a thread consistency. Let cool. Mix the yolks and the white, making sure the mixture is uniform but not frothy. Cover the sides and bottom of the mold with the syrup. Pour the syrup over the eggs and mix with a wooden spoon. Place the mixture in the mold. Cover with aluminum foil and steam in a pressure cooker, or in a normal saucepan, placing a bowl on the bottom to prevent the water from touching the mold, and place the egg shells in the water in order to keep it from splashing up. Cover well and cook for 20 minutes, counted as from the moment the water comes to a boil. Cook only for 8 minutes if using a pressure cooker. Remove from the mold when cool.
