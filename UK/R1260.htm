   FOR 2 SERVINGS

2 partridges

125 cc of oil

1 teaspoon of vinegar

2 heads of garlic

2 onions

Black pepper

Clove

Thyme

Sweet Marjoram

Salt

Clean the partridges. Place in a pan with the whole unpeeled garlic cloves, the onions cut in rings, the crushed pepper and clove, and the chopped thyme and sweet marjoram. Add the salt and sprinkle with vinegar, oil and 250 cc of water. Cover well and cook at a low flame for 35 minutes, moving the pan occasionally.
