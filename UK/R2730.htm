   FOR 12 UNITS

1/2 kg of puff pastry.

1/2 l of cooking oil.

200 g of sugar.

For the cream:

250 cc of milk.

4 egg yolks

100 g of sugar.

1 tablespoon of cornstarch.

Grated lemon peel.

Cinnamon Powder.

For the cream, heat the milk with the cinnamon, the lemon and the sugar. Beat the yolks and mix with the cornstarch. Add the milk and stir until the mixture thickens. Sprinkle a flat surface with flour and spread out the mixture with a rolling pin. Cut out 24 circles. Place a spoonful of cream onto half of the circles. Dampen the edges of the remaining circles and place them firmly on top of the ones with the cream. Heat the oil in a deep pan and fry on both sides. Place on absorbent paper and sprinkle with sugar.
