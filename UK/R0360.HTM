Stuffed Cabbage

   FOR 4 SERVINGS

1 medium cabbage

200 g of cooked rice

200 g of lean bacon

1 medium onion

4-5 cloves of garlic

2 egg yolks

100 g of bread without crust

Parsley

Cinnamon

Saffron

1 l of beef stock (you can use cubes)

250 cc of white wine

100 g of butter

Make an opening in the cabbage by carefully hollowing out the center. Mix the rice with the very finely chopped onion, the ground bacon, crushed garlic, parsley, cinnamon, saffron and mashed bread. Mix these ingredients with the egg yolks, fill the cabbage and pack well. Place the cabbage in a pan with the hollowed out part up. Add the beef stock, the wine and the butter. Cook at a low flame for one hour.
