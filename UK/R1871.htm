   FOR 50 UNITS

300 cc of cream

10 g of bicarbonate of soda.

2 eggs.

250 g of sugar

1 tablespoonful of aniseed.

450 g of flour.

Mix the cream and the bicarbonate of soda. Leave for 10 minutes. Beat the eggs as if making an omelette and mix with the cream. Add the sugar, the aniseed and the flour, and mix well. Sprinkle flour on your hands and give shape to the "rosquetes". Place in floured pie pans. Place in a hot oven and bake for 25 minutes.
