   FOR 10 UNITS

250 g of flour

300 g of sugar

2 eggs

2 egg yolks

Aniseed

Musk

Rose water

Beat the whole eggs and the egg yolks, as if for an omelette. Mix with the sugar and the flour. Crush a tablespoon of aniseed with a pinch of musk. Add a tablespoon of rose water. Mix with the batter. Form small cakes in any shape you want, but make sure they are thin. Bake for 20 minutes at a medium temperature.
