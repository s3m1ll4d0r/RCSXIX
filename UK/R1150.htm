Rabbit in Boiled "Mojo" Sauce

   FOR 4 SERVINGS

1 kg of rabbit

250 cc of oil

250 cc of white wine

3 tablespoons of vinegar

8-10 cloves of garlic

100 g of stale or oven-hardened bread

1 tablespoon of saffron

1 teaspoon of ground black pepper

Clean the rabbit and cut into pieces. Cook in the oven at a high temperature for 10 minutes. Meanwhile, mix the rest of the ingredients together, with a beater. Place the rabbit in a pan and pour the sauce over it. Cook at a low temperature for 45 minutes.
