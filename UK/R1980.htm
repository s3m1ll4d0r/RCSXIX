   FOR 12 UNITS

12 egg yolks.

400 g of sugar.

1 teaspoon of cinnamon.

1 teaspoon of grated lemon peel.

Make a syrup with the lemon and the cinnamon, to the consistency of a thick thread. Beat the yolks very well. Separate the syrup into two halves, and let cool slightly. Place the yolks in one of the two halves and mix very well. Place on the flame and cook, while stirring all the time. Let cool and form little balls. Cook the remaining syrup till it achieves the consistency of clear caramel. Pass the yolks through this syrup and place on a greased baking sheet. Serve cool.
