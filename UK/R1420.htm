   FOR 6 SERVINGS

125 cc of white wine

125 cc of oil

1 teaspoon of salt

300 g of flour

2 tablespoons of sugar

2 egg yolks

125 cc of wine (Port or similar)

1 teaspoon of cinnamon

Filling:

100 g of lean bacon

1 tomato

1 onion

200 g of cooked veal

Parsley

To prepare the dough first mix the white wine, the oil and the salt together. Add the flour and knead well. Cover with a damp cloth and let stand for one hour. Prepare the filling: Mince the bacon and the onion very fine. Brown at a low flame. When the onion is tender add the peeled and seeded tomato. When the tomato is well reduced, add the meat, chopped in very small pieces. Brown and add the parsley. Divide the dough into two pieces and spread out with a rolling pin, on a well floured surface. Preheat the oven to a medium temperature. Place one of the pieces of dough on a baking pan, sprinkled with flour. Put the filling in the center, leaving two centimeters at the edges. Moisten the edges with water and place the other piece of dough on top. Close the edges well folding one edge over the other. Bake in the oven for 20 minutes. Meanwhile, prepare the topping by beating the egg yolks with the sugar, the wine and the cinnamon. Spread half of the mixture on the pie and bake for 15 minutes. Spread on another coat and bake for another 15 minutes. It can be served hot or cold.
