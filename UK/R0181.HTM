Stuffed Eggplant

   FOR 4 SERVINGS

4 eggplants

2 tablespoons of bread crumbs

100 g of grated cheese

4-5 cloves of garlic

2 eggs

125 cc of wine

Cumin

Salt

Sweet marjoram

Cover the egg plant with water and boil. When they are tender, drain and cut lengthwise. Take out the inside part leaving 1/2 cm of pulp stuck to the skin. Prepare the stuffing by mashing the pulp of the eggplant and mixing it with the cheese, the bread, the spices, the pressed garlic, the egg yolks and the two tablespoons of oil. Fill the eggplant with the mixture. Heat the oil. Beat the egg whites, dip the eggplant in them and fry face down for two minutes. Soak up the excess oil on absorbent paper, place them in a baking pan and bake for 10 minutes at a medium temperature.
