Rosví (Roastbeef)

   FOR 10 SERVINGS

2 1/2 kg of beef back

4 lemons

100 g of flour

100 g of cooking fat

250 cc of white wine

Salt

Pepper

2 tablespoons of oil

Make a paste with the flour, the lemon juice, the salt and the pepper. Coat the meat and let stand for at least one hour. Put the cooking fat, the oil, the white wine and the meat in a pot with a lid. Cover and roast at a low heat, for 1 1/2 hours turning the meat every 20 minutes.
