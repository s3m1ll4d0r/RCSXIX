   FOR 50 UNITS

2 eggs

200 g of sugar

100 g of flour

2 tablespoons of orange-flower water

Oil to grease the baking sheets

Thoroughly mix the eggs, without beating, with the flour, the sugar and the orange-flower water. Preheat the oven. Cover the baking sheets with parchment paper which has been greased with oil. Place the batter on the baking sheets with a small spoon making sure there is plenty of separation. Bake for 15 minutes at a medium temperature.
