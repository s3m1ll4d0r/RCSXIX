Marinated Beef Stew

Index

   FOR 4 SERVINGS

400 g of beef

4 cloves of garlic

1 small onion

2 tomatoes

250 cc of oil

Parsley

Ground Black Pepper

Nutmeg

For the marinade: Salt

Laurel

Sweet marjoram

2 tablespoons of oil

Prepare the marinade and place the meat in it, cut into cubes. Let stand for at least one hour. Fry the meat and add the rest of the ingredients, previously beaten. Cover and cook at a low flame for 30 minutes, adding a few tablespoons of water if necessary.
