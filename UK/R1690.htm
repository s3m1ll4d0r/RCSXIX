Fresh Salted Mackerels

   FOR 4 SERVINGS

200 g of rice

2 medium sized mackerels

1 onion

100 cc of oil

1 lemon

Cinnamon

Clove

Saffron

Parsley

Salt

Wash the mackerels and cut them into round slices, setting the heads and tails aside for later. Mince the onion and brown in the oil. Add the rice and brown lightly. Add water (double the amount of rice). Place the heads and tails in a strainer or and place it in the pan, so that the heads and tails cook in the water with the rice, for 10 minutes, at a low flame. Crush the saffron with the clove and combine with the ground cinnamon and salt. Remove the strainer with the fish heads and tails and add the rest of the fish. Cook for another 10 minutes. Set aside, add lemon juice and let stand for five minutes before serving.
