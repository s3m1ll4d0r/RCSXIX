   FOR 12 SERVINGS

1 chicken

1 kg of soup beef

1/2 kg of calf or lamb feet

2 carrots

1 onion

1 head of garlic

Clove

Black pepper

Lemon peel

Cinnamon

Nutmeg

Salt

Sugar

Serve with:

2 ripe tomatoes

Mint leaves

Place all of the ingredients in a large pot, except for the salt and the sugar. Add 10 l of cold water. Boil for at least 4 hours, skimming whenever necessary. The broth should be reduced by half and the meat well cooked. Strain the broth with a very fine mesh strainer. Add the salt and sugar to your taste. Strain the broth again through a clean cloth, folded in four, and wetted with cold water in order to remove the fat. Strain once again with another clean cloth folded in four, pouring little by little so that it passes through the cloth slowly, in order to remove all impurities. Repeat the procedure if necessary. Let it set in the refrigerator for at least 4 hours. Serve cold, mixed with pieces of tomato, peeled and seeded, and very finely chopped mint leaves.
