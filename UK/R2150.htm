"Besitos" (Little kisses)

   FOR 6 SERVINGS

2 eggs.

100 cc of oil.

2 apples.

200 g of sugar.

100 g of flour.

1 level teaspoon of cinnamon.

Lemon peel.

1 litre of cooking oil.

Fry the lemon peel with the 100 cc of oil until well brown. Make a syrup with sugar and 250 cc of water. Peel the apple and cut into small cubes. Mix the flour with warm oil, grated lemon peel and the unbeaten eggs. Mix well and add the apple cubes. Heat the oil and place teaspoonfuls of the batter in it. Remove from the oil, place on absorbent paper. Place in the boiling syrup, turn them a few times and remove. Serve hot or warm.
