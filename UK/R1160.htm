Stuffed Chicken with Almonds

   FOR 4 SERVINGS

1 chicken

200 g of lean bacon

100 g of almonds

20 g of stale or oven-hardened bread

125 cc of white wine

125 cc of chicken stock

4 cloves of garlic

1 level tablespoon of sugar

1/2 level tablespoon of cinnamon

Thyme

Sage

Prepare the stuffing with the bacon, the almonds, the bread, the cinnamon and a little sugar, all finely minced or ground. Clean the chicken and stuff it with this mixture. Sew it shut so that the stuffing does not come out, and tie the legs and wings. Set the oven to a high temperature. Place the chicken in a baking pan with the garlic around it. When the garlic is toasted, take it out and crush it with a mortar. Reduce the temperature of the oven. Boil the wine with the chicken stock, add the crushed garlic, cinnamon, thyme and sage. Use this to baste the chicken occasionally. Roast for one hour at a medium temperature.
