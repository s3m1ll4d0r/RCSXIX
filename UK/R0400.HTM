   FOR 4 SERVINGS

1 l of beef stock

8 eggs

4 tablespoons of sherry

4 tablespoons of brown sugar

Cinnamon

Skim off the fat of the beef stock and let cool. Beat the eggs. Add the stock and the sherry and mix well. Put the mixture in custard molds, for serving later, and cover with aluminum foil. Double boil at a low flame. When the mixture sets, sprinkle with brown sugar and cinnamon. Serve cold.
