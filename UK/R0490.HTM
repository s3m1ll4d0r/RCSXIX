Meatball Soup

   FOR 6 SERVINGS

1 1/2 l of beef stock

200 g of boiled beef

2 eggs

200 g of potatoes

1 tomato

2 cloves of garlic

1/2 l of oil

Parsley

Cut the potatoes into small round slices. Heat the oil and fry the potatoes. Set them aside. Mince the beef and mix it with the parsley and the egg yolks. Make the meatballs very small. Beat the egg whites lightly and dip the meatballs in them. Fry them in the same oil in which the potatoes were fried. Set the meatballs aside. Pour the oil out of the frying pan, leaving just a fine film in which you fry the onion, very finely chopped, the garlic, also finely chopped, and the peeled and seeded tomato. When the tomato is well reduced add the fried ingredients with the beef stock and boil all together. Add the potatoes and the meatballs and cook at a low flame for 3 or 4 minutes.
