   FOR 6 SERVINGS

1 onion

2 tablespoons of oil

2 eggs

1 1/2 l of beef or chicken stock

6 slices of toasted bread

Parsley

Finely chop the onion and the parsley. Fry together in the oil until the onion softens. Add the stock. Boil for 10 minutes. Place the bread in a heat resistant soup bowl. Beat the eggs with 2 tablespoons of the hot beef or chicken stock. Pour over the bread. Add the rest of the stock. Place in a hot oven for 5 minutes.
