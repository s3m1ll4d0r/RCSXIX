Chocolate with Egg

   FOR 4 SERVINGS

1/2 l of milk

200 g of dark chocolate

2 tablespoons of sugar

2 eggs

Grate the chocolate. Heat the milk with the chocolate and the sugar and stir with a wooden spoon. Beat the egg whites till stiff and add the yolks. When the chocolate begins to boil, start adding the eggs slowly, without beating. Serve immediately.
