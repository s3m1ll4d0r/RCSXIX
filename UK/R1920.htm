"Huevos Moles" (dessert of egg yolk and sugar).

   FOR 8 SERVINGS

250 g of sugar.

12 egg yolks.

1 egg white.

Lemon peel.

1 small stick of cinnamon.

Make a syrup using the sugar, the lemon peel, the cinnamon and 250 cc of water until a thread consistency is obtained. Let cool and remove the cinnamon and the lemon peel. Beat the yolks until they are white and frothy. Beat the egg white till stiff, add to the yolks and slowly, while beating, pour onto the cold syrup. Double boil while beating with a whisk until thick. Remove from the flame and continue beating for at least 2 hours, if done by hand, or 20-30 minutes with an electric beater, until cold. Serve in small dessert cups.
