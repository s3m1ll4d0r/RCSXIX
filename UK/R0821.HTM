Roasted Red Mullet

   FOR 6 SERVINGS

6 red mullets

6 cloves of garlic

1 tablespoon of breadcrumbs

125 cc of oil

Parsley

Salt

Parchment paper or aluminum foil

Preheat the oven. Coat the fish with oil after cleaning them. Sprinkle with the breadcrumbs, parsley and salt. Wrap each fish in the paper or foil coated with oil and close well. Place in a baking pan and bake in the oven for 20 minutes at a high temperature. Meanwhile, chop the garlic and fry in the oil. Remove the fish, open the wrapping and place the fried garlic chips on top.
