Milanese Rice Cake

   FOR 8 SERVINGS

250 g of rice

300 cc of water

1/2 l of milk

125 cc of rum

250 g of sugar

100 g of raisins

4 eggs

Grated lemon peels

Butter (to grease the mold)

Soak the raisins in the rum. Cook the rice in the water. When the water begins to evaporate, add the milk and cook at a low flame. Preheat the oven. Grease the mold with the butter. Beat the eggs with the sugar. Add the rice, the raisins and rum and the grated lemon peel. Place the mixture in the mold and bake for 35 minutes at a medium temperature.
