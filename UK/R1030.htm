Stuffed Roasted Chicken

   FOR 4 SERVINGS

1 chicken

200 g of lean bacon

100 g of "jamón serrano" (cured Spanish ham) or Parma ham

50 g of almonds

50 g of olives

2 hard-boiled eggs

Clean the chicken without breaking the skin. Preheat the oven. Prepare the stuffing by mincing the ham, the pit less olives, the almonds and the hard-boiled eggs. Fill the chicken with this mixture. Sew the opening shut and tie the legs and wings. Place in a baking pan and cover with the bacon cut in long strips. Place in the oven and cook at a medium temperature for 1 hour 45 minutes.
