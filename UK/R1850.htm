Bienmesabe (Almond Dessert)

   FOR 4 SERVINGS

200 g of almonds

200 g of sugar

4 egg yolks

1 cinnamon stick

Lemon peel

Make a syrup with the sugar, the cinnamon stick, the lemon peel and 250 cc of water. When it obtains a thread consistency, remove the lemon and the cinnamon, and add the almonds. Continue cooking over a low flame. Beat the yolks slightly and add to the almonds. When thick, serve in dessert cups.
