   FOR 6 SERVINGS

1 dentex or sea bream (approx. 1,200 g)

100 g of chopped almonds

1 hard-boiled egg

12 olives without pits

6 cloves of garlic

1 tablespoon of breadcrumbs

1 onion

1 tomato

100 cc of oil

250 cc cup of white wine

125 cc cup of fish stock

Salt

Clean the fish, cut it open and remove the bone. Wash it well and sprinkle with salt. Heat the oil and fry the garlic. Remove and set aside. Fry the chopped almonds in the same oil. Remove and set aside also. Fry the very finely chopped onion and add the peeled and seeded tomato. When the tomato is reduced to a thick sauce, add the almonds, the chopped olives, the hard-boiled egg, also chopped, and the crushed fried garlic. Mix with the 2 tablespoons of wine. Fill the fish with this mixture. Sew up the opening so that the filling is not lost. Place on a roasting pan. Mix what is left of the filling with the wine and the broth. Pour over the top of the fish and bake in a hot oven for 25 minutes.
