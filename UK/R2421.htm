   FOR 10 UNITS

100 g of crushed almonds

250 g of sugar

Cinnamon powder

Aniseed

Make a syrup to a thread consistency using the sugar and 250 cc of water. Add the almonds, stirring with a wooden spoon until it thickens. Remove from the flame and add the cinnamon and the crushed aniseed. Spread the dough out over a wet board. Cut into rectangles before it cools.
