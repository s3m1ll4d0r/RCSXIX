   FOR 400 cc

1/2 l of white wine vinegar

10 g of absinthe

10 g of rosemary

10 g of sage

10 g of mint

10 g of rue

10 g of lavender

1 g of camphor

2 tablespoons of alcohol

Soak the herbs in the vinegar, in a closed container, for 10 days. Strain and mix with the alcohol in which the camphor is dissolved. Keep closed. Use for massages.
