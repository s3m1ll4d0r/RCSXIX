Jamaican Style Banana Soup

   FOR 4 SERVINGS

6 bananas.

1/2 glass of rum.

200 g of sugar

1 cinnamon stick.

In a large frying pan, large enough for the bananas to have plenty of space between, make a syrup with the sugar, a half a glass of water and the cinnamon. When you obtain a thread consistency, add the rum. Boil on a low flame for 10 minutes. Add the peeled bananas. Turn them occasionally, until cooked but firm. Serve hot or cold with ice cream.
