Buns or "Nuns' Sigh"

   FOR 12 UNITS

50 g of butter.

100 g of sugar.

100 g of flour.

3 eggs.

Melt the butter with the sugar and 250 cc of water. Sift the flour. When the water comes to a boil, remove from the flame and add the flour all at once. Mix with a wooden spoon and return to the flame, and continue stirring until the batter becomes unstuck from the sides of the saucepan. Remove from the flame and add the whole eggs, one by one, mixing each one thoroughly before adding the next. Preheat the oven to a medium temperature. Sprinkle a baking sheet with flour, place evenly spaced spoonfuls of the batter on it and bake for 20 minutes. Remove from the oven and sprinkle with sugar or fill the buns with cream or marmalade.
