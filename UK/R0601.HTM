Rice or Noodle Soup

   FOR 4 SERVINGS

2 onions

10 cloves of garlic

100 cc of oil

150 g of noodles

1 l of beef stock

100 g of grated cheese

Parsley

Saffron

Paprika

Black pepper

Mince the onions very fine and brown in the hot oil. Add the chopped garlic and parsley. When the onions are tender add the spices and the beef stock. When it begins to boil add the noodles and cook over a moderate flame for 15 minutes. Mix it with the grated cheese upon serving.
