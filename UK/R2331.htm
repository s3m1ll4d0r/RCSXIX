   FOR 6 SERVINGS

250 g of dark chocolate

200 g of confectioners' sugar

4 egg whites

4 tablespoons of milk

Melt the chocolate in the milk over a low flame, stirring with a wooden spoon. When it becomes creamy remove from the flame and add the sugar, saving a tablespoon for later. Let cool a little. At the same time beat the egg whites till stiff. Add the tablespoon of sugar to the egg whites, to prevent them from falling. Mix the egg whites with the melted chocolate using a wooden spoon. Place in a dessert cup and serve cold.
