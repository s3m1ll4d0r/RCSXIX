   FOR 6 SERVINGS

3 egg yolks.

100 g of dark chocolate.

100 g of sugar.

50 g of flour

1 glass of milk.

Butter for the mold.

Grease 6 individual molds. Preheat the oven. Melt the chocolate with the milk. Add the flour and mix with a beater until the mixture thickens. Beat the yolks with the sugar until very frothy. Add the chocolate mixture while beating constantly. Pour the mixture into the molds, place in the oven and double boil at a moderate temperature for 15 minutes. Serve cold with cream.
