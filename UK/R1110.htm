Marinated Pork Chops

   FOR 6 SERVINGS

6 pork chops

1 lemon

Parsley

Oregano

Thyme

Pepper

Salt

100 cc of oil

Squeeze the lemon and cut the peel in strips. Mix with the oil and the spices. Coat the pork chops with this mixture, cover and leave in the upper part of the refrigerator for at least 24 hours. Fry in a non-stick frying pan for 3 minutes on each side. Sprinkle with salt and pepper when they are done.
