   For 1/2 Kg of boiled soup beef

125 cc of white wine

125 cc of oil

250 cc of broth

1 slice of bread

1 teaspoon of sugar

4 cloves of garlic

1 small hot red pepper

Parsley

Soak the bread with the wine, the oil and the sugar. Crush the garlic with the parsley and the red pepper. Blend the bread, the garlic and the broth with a beater or blender. Baste the meat with this sauce while it bakes in the oven at a high temperature for 20 minutes. Serve the sauce hot, with the meat.
