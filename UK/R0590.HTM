Green Bean Stew

   FOR 8 SERVINGS

1/2 kg of green beans

2 tablespoons of flour

4 tablespoons of oil

2 onions

4 eggs

2 cubes of vegetable bouillon

Parsley

Salt

Cover the green beans with water and cook with the cubes of vegetable bouillon. When they are tender drain the water and set it aside for later. Cut the onions into thin strips and fry them in the oil. When they are translucent add the flour and stir with a wooden spoon. Gradually add the water in which the green beans were cooked and continue stirring. When the liquid comes to a boil and thickens a little add the green beans and the chopped parsley. Just before serving add the beaten eggs and boil for 1 minute.
