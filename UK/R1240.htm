Ham Bread

   FOR 6 SERVINGS

500 g of flour

10 g of baking powder

500 g of cooked ham, in slices

2 tablespoons of milk

Sesame seeds

2 tablespoons of oil

Tomato sauce

Sage

Prepare the bread dough by dissolving the baking powder with a few tablespoons of warm water and mixing with flour. Knead well and let stand for one hour. Preheat the oven. Knead again and divide the dough into four pieces and spread out. Grease a baking sheet with oil. Put the first layer of dough on the sheet and a third of the ham on top. Continue the same procedure with the dough and the ham, finishing with the dough on top and making sure that the edges are well stuck together. Place the bread in the oven, preheated to medium temperature, for 45 minutes. Coat the bread with the milk and put the sesame seeds on top. Set the oven to the maximum temperature and bake the bread for 5 minutes or until it is brown on top. Serve it with tomato sauce with a few chopped sage leaves added.
