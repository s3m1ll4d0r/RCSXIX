   FOR 4 SERVINGS

12 sponge fingers (see the recipe "Simple sponge fingers....").

150 g of sugar.

4 egg whites.

1 lemon.

Use the sugar, the lemon peel and 125 cc of water to make a syrup of a thread consistency. Let cool. Beat the egg whites till stiff. Mix with the syrup and continue beating for 4-5 minutes. Place the sponge fingers in a baking pan and sprinkle them with the lemon juice. Preheat the oven to a high temperature. Distribute the meringue over the sponge fingers. Place in the oven and bake for 5 minutes, or until brown on top. Serve cold.
