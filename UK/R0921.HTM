   FOR 4 SERVINGS

1 kg of octopus

1 onion

4-5 cloves of garlic

250 cc of white wine

Cut the octopus into small pieces. Place in a pan with the onion, cut in thin strips, and the garlic. Cover with the white wine and water. Cook at a moderate flame for 55-60 minutes, until tender. If you use a pressure cooker, add just the wine and no water and let cook for 15-18 minutes.
