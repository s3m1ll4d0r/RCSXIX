Fish with Rice

Index

   FOR 6 SERVINGS

400 g of clean white fish

250 g of rice

1 onion

1 tomato

4 cloves of garlic

125 cc of oil

1/2 l of fish stock (you can use cubes)

1 cooked red pepper

2 hard-boiled eggs

Saffron

Parsley

Dry the fish and cut it into pieces. Heat half of the oil and fry the garlic and the fish in it. Remove and set aside. Brown the rice in the same oil. Add the fish stock. Meanwhile, heat the rest of the oil and fry the minced onion and the parsley. Crush the fried garlic with the saffron. Peel the tomatoes and mince them. Add the fried onion, the crushed garlic, the tomato and the fish to the rice. Finally, simmer at a low flame. Garnish with egg slices and red pepper strips.
