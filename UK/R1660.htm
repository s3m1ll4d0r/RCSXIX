   FOR 30 UNITS

200 g of boiled cod

100 g of boiled potatoes

1 tablespoon of flour

4 eggs

2 cloves of garlic

1 tablespoon of white wine

Parsley

1/2 l of oil (for frying)

Cut the cod into pieces, removing the skin and bones. Mix with the potatoes which have been previously broken up with a fork. Crush the garlic with the parsley, mix with the wine and add to the potatoes. Add salt if necessary. Beat the eggs, as if for an omelette, and mix with the cod batter, adding flour. Heat the oil in a deep frying pan. Fry small spoonful of the batter in the hot oil. Fry on both sides until brown. Place on absorbent paper. Serve hot.
