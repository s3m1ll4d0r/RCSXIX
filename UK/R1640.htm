Marinated Sea Bream

   FOR 4 SERVINGS

1 kg of sea bream

1/2 l of oil

2 lemons

125 cc of vinegar

Laurel

Salt

Clean the fish and, if it is a large one, cut it into pieces. Dry well and sprinkle with salt. Fry the fish in hot oil. Remove the fish and place in a glass or porcelain cooking pan. Let the oil cool a little, add the vinegar, 250 cc of water, some laurel leaves and cook at a low flame for 3 or 4 minutes. Cover the fish with the lemons cut in round slices and pour the sauce over it. Cover and serve when completely chilled.
