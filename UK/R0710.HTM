Rice Ring

   FOR 8 SERVINGS

2 onions

1 tomato

4 cloves of garlic

1 red pepper

200 g of rice

100 g of grated cheese

300 g of peas

2 tablespoons of butter

4 tablespoons of oil

Parsley

Sugar

Heat the oil and fry the well minced onions and garlic until translucent. Cut the peeled and seeded tomatoes into pieces and add to the onions and garlic. Also add the red pepper, cut into squares. Fry until the tomato is reduced to a sauce. Add the rice and turn it several times. Add water (double the volume of the rice). Before the water totally evaporates, mix in the grated cheese, remove from the flame and cover. Cook the peas and when they are tender add the butter, the chopped parsley and a pinch of sugar. Place the rice in a ring mold. Put it in a very hot oven for a few minutes. Remove the mold and serve with the peas in the center.
