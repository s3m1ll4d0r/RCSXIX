   FOR 6 SERVINGS

800 g of peeled apples.

150 g of butter.

200 g of sugar.

100 g of crystalized fruit.

6 eggs.

Cinnamon

Nutmeg.

Cook the apples in two tablespoons of water, until they break up. Beat the butter with the sugar and add the cold apples. Beat the eggs, whites and yolks together. Add to the apples, with the cinnamon, the nutmeg and the fruit cut into small portions. Preheat the oven. Grease the mold with butter. Place the mixture in the mold and double boil in the oven for 45 minutes at a medium temperature.
