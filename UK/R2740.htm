   FOR 6 SERVINGS

335 g of sugar.

225 g of grounds almonds.

4 yolks.

Lemon peel.

Cinnamon.

Make a syrup to a thick thread consistency using the sugar, 250 cc of water, the lemon peel and a stick of cinnamon. Strain and add the ground almonds. Place over the flame and stir with a wooden spoon until the almonds are cooked (about 10 minutes). Add the beaten yolks and continue stirring until the mixture thickens. Pour into a pan while stirring slightly until it is cool enough to touch with the fingers. Place in a mold and press down firmly so that no air is trapped inside the batter. Remove from the mold after 24 hours.
