   FOR 4 SERVINGS

1/2 litre of milk.

3 egg yolks.

1 tablespoon of rice flour.

250 g of sugar.

Grated lemon peel.

Cinnamon Powder

Heat the milk with 150 g of sugar, the grated lemon peel and the cinnamon. Beat the yolks and mix with the rice flour. Add spoonful of hot milk to the yolks while mixing with a wooden spoon until the milk has been consumed. Place on the flame and mix until thick. Distribute the cream on small plates and sprinkle with the rest of the sugar. Heat an iron utensil to a red hot temperature and burn the sugar.
