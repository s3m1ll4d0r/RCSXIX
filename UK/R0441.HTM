Macaroni Pie

   FOR 4 SERVINGS

2 chicken breasts

250 g of macaroni

200 g of grated cheese

2 hard-boiled eggs

2 tablespoons of bread crumbs

1 tablespoon of butter or margarine

1/4 l of tomato sauce

Salt

Cook the chicken breasts in the sauce. Take them out and cut them into small pieces. Cook the macaroni with plenty of water and add salt. When the macaroni is tender drain it and mix it with the sauce. Grease a deep baking pan with butter. Place a layer of macaroni in it, half of the chicken and the hard-boiled eggs in round slices. Cover with the rest of the macaroni and chicken. Sprinkle with bread crumbs. Put the rest of the butter on top and bake for 10 minutes at a high temperature.
