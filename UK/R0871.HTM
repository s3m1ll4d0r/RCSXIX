Fish Stew without Water

   FOR 4 SERVINGS

1 dentex (approx. 1,200 g) (known locally as sama)

1 kg of onion

1 kg of tomato

6 cloves of garlic

2 tablespoons of vinegar

100 cc of oil

Parsley

Paprika

Salt

Black pepper

1 lemon

Cut the onions and the tomatoes into round slices. Place half of the onions at the bottom of a large pan. Put half of the tomatoes on top of the onions and the fish on top of the tomatoes. Cover with the rest of the onions and tomatoes. Add the chopped garlic, the parsley, salt, paprika and black pepper. Pour the oil, vinegar and lemon juice on top. Cover well and cook at a low flame for 35 minutes, until the vegetables are tender. Serve hot or cold.
