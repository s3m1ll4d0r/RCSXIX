   FOR 6 SERVINGS

450 g of sugar.

4 large lemons.

4 eggs.

Make a light syrup with the sugar and 250 cc of water. When the syrup cools, beat the eggs (whites and yolks together) until frothy. Add the syrup and continue stirring for 30 minutes if by hand, or 15 minutes with an electric beater. Add the juice of the lemons and place on the flame while stirring all the time until it thickens. Serve cold in small dessert cups.
