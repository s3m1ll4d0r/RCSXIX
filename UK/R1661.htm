Lobster with Garlic and Lemon

Previous

Index 

Next

 

 

 

   FOR 4 SERVINGS

2 lobsters

1 lemon

6 cloves of garlic

125 cc of oil

Ground black pepper

Salt

Cook the lobsters in plenty of boiling water (salted) for 10 minutes. Let the water cool. Take out the lobsters, cut open, remove the meat and cut it into pieces. Chop the garlic and mix it with the oil, the lemon and the pepper, using a fork. Pour over the lobster. Let cool in the refrigerator.
