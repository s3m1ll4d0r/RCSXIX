Fried Rosquetes

   FOR 25 UNITS

200 g of flour.

300 g of sugar.

2 eggs.

4 tablespoonfuls of a liqueur (Cointreau, Annisette, etc).

1/2 litre of cooking oil.

Make a syrup with the sugar and 125 cc of water. Keep hot. Beat the egg whites till stiff. Mix with the yolks. Add the flour. Sprinkle your hands with flour and give shape to the "rosquetes". Heat the oil and fry the "rosquetes". As you remove them from the oil, place in the syrup and boil for 1 minute, while pouring the syrup over them with a spoon. Remove and place on a tray, well spaced apart, and let dry.
