   FOR 4 SERVINGS

1 kg of veal steaks

200 g of stale or oven-hardened bread

250 g of lean bacon

1 tablespoon of chopped parsley

1 small onion

4 cloves of garlic

150 g of green olives

125 cc of oil

250 cc of broth

125 cc of white wine

2 laurel leaves

1/2 teaspoon of salt

1/2 teaspoon of pepper

Make a paste with the bread, parsley, onion, bacon, garlic and half of the olives. Use this mixture to coat the steaks, which you will later roll and tie. Fry in the oil and place in a pan with the wine, the broth, the salt, pepper, laurel and chopped olives. Simmer until the sauce is a little bit thick.
