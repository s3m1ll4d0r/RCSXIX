   FOR 4 SERVINGS

1 kg of artichokes

1 onion

2 tomatoes

4 cloves of garlic

250 cc of white wine

125 cc of oil

Parsley

Black Pepper

Saffron

Lemon juice

Salt

Clean the artichokes, Cut off the tips of the leaves and the bottom to remove the fibers. Wash with water and lemon juice. Heat the oil. Cut the onion in thin strips. Peel and seed the tomatoes. Cut the garlic in round slices. Fry everything together in the oil and add the parsley, black pepper, salt and crushed saffron. When the sauce thickens a little add the artichokes. Simmer and add the wine. Cook at a low flame, adding a little water if necessary. Remove from the flame when the artichokes are tender.
