   FOR 8 SERVINGS

800 g of cauliflower

4 eggs

1/2 l of milk

1/2 l of cream

100 g of bread without the crust

Nutmeg

Soak the bread in the milk and cream. Cook the cauliflower and drain. Reduce the bread, milk, cream and cauliflower to a puree. Add the egg yolks. Beat the egg whites till stiff and mix with the puree. Grease a mold with butter and coat with bread crumbs. Put the mixture in the mold and double boil in the oven for 90 minutes at a medium temperature.
