   FOR 20 UNITS

250 g of ground almonds.

350 g of confectioners' sugar.

2 egg whites.

Cinnamon powder.

Crushed aniseed

The almonds must be very well ground. Mix them with 250 g of sugar with the cinnamon and the aniseed, and add the unbeaten egg whites by the spoonful until you obtain a fine paste which can be shaped with your hands. Make the shapes and place on a baking sheet. Sprinkle with sugar and bake for 25 minutes at a moderate temperature. Remove from the oven and sprinkle with the rest of the sugar. Leave on the baking sheet to cool.
