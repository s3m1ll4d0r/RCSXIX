Potato or Yam Pudding

   FOR 8 SERVINGS

1 kg of cooked yams.

1/2 litre of milk.

10 sponge fingers

150 g of corinth raisins.

100 g of roasted and chopped hazelnuts.

1/2 glass of sweet wine.

6 eggs.

3 tablespoons of lemon marmalade or pumpkin pulp

Nutmeg.

Cinnamon Powder

Grated lemon peel.

Butter for the mold.

Confectioners' sugar mixed with cinnamon for the topping

Soak the sponge fingers in the wine. Meanwhile peel the yams and cut them into small cubes. Place in a saucepan and over the flame with the milk, the lemon peel, the cinnamon and the grated nutmeg. Cook until well done. Preheat the oven. Grease the mold. Puree the mixture of the yams, the milk and the sponge fingers using a blender or electric beater. Add the eggs one by one. Mix with the raisins, the hazelnuts and the lemon marmalade. Pour in the mold and bake for 40 minutes at a medium temperature. Let cool in the mold, remove and sprinkle with the ground sugar mixed with cinnamon.
