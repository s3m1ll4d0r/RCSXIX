   FOR 4 SERVINGS

250 g of beans

300 g of potatoes

1 tomato

1 onion

100 cc of oil

Salt

Paprika

Thyme

Oregano

Boil the beans in water alone. Meanwhile, fry the finely minced onion, the peeled and seeded tomato, the parsley, thyme, oregano and paprika in the oil. Add this to the beans. Cut the potatoes into cubes. When the beans begin to get tender add the potatoes and boil everything together.
