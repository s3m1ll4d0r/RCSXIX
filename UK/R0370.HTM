Chick-pea Stew

   FOR 8 SERVINGS

1/2 kg of chick-peas

2 eggs

2 bouillon cubes

125 cc of milk

125 cc of white wine

50 g of crushed almonds

50 g of seedless raisins

Cover the chick-peas with cold water and boil with the two bouillon cubes. Meanwhile, hard boil the eggs. Soak the raisins in the wine. Chop the hard-boiled eggs in very small pieces and mix with the raisins, the wine, the milk and the almonds. Stir in while heating the mixture until it comes to a boil. When the chick-peas are tender add the sauce and boil for a few minutes.
