   FOR 10 UNITS

200 cc of cream

2 eggs

2 tablespoons of sugar

200 g of flour

1 teaspoon of bicarbonate of soda

1/2 teaspoon of salt

Mix the cream with the bicarbonate of soda. Let stand for 15 minutes. Preheat the oven. Add the whole eggs, without beating, one by one, and mix well. Add the salt and the sugar. Sift the flour and mix with the rest. Sprinkle the baking sheets with flour. Form the cakes with a large spoon and place them on the baking sheets. Bake for 20 minutes at a medium temperature.
