   FOR 4 SERVINGS

1/2 kg of cooked veal backs (leftover)

2 eggs

1 tablespoon of flour

2 tablespoons of vinegar

Parsley

Ground black pepper

Salt

250 cc of oil

Cut the meat in thin strips. Finely chop the parsley and mix with salt, pepper and vinegar. Coat the meat with this mixture and let stand. Heat the oil. Beat the eggs as if you were making an omelette, and mix them with the flour. Dip the meat in the batter and fry for one minute on each side. Serve with lemon sauce (see the corresponding recipe).
