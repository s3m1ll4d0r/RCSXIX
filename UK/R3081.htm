Pickled Cucumber, Green Beans and Chayotes

   FOR 2000 cc

2 cucumbers

4 chayotes

250 g of green beans

2 l of vinegar

1 tablespoon of salt

1 tablespoon of black pepper

1 tablespoon of clove

1 tablespoon of ginger powder (or a piece)

1 hot pepper

1 nutmeg

Peel the cucumbers and the chayotes. Remove the ends and the string from the green beans. Cover the vegetables with water, add salt and boil until tender. Remove from the water and let drain on a clean cloth. When they are dry enough place in an earthenware casserole dish, with the green beans tied, the cucumbers cut in thick round slices and the chayotes cut lengthwise in four pieces, with the seeds removed. Add the spices, the pepper and the whole cloves, a piece of ginger (or ginger powder), the hot pepper and the nutmeg in large pieces. Pour the vinegar on top. Put over a low flame until it comes to a boil. Remove from the flame and cover for 24 hours. The following day drain the vinegar into another pan and boil for 2 or 3 minutes. Pour over the vegetables again. Repeat the operation for 4 days. On the fifth day put the vegetables in sealed containers, distributing the vinegar and the spices equally.
