   FOR 4 SERVINGS

4 thick steaks

250 cc of broth

125 cc of wine

2 slices of toasted bread

6 cloves of garlic

1/2 teaspoon of salt

1/2 teaspoon of pepper

2 tablespoons of oil

Heat the oil and fry the meat for one minute on each side. Remove the steaks and in the same oil fry the garlic. Remove and set aside when done. Make a paste with the fried garlic, the bread, the broth and the wine. Season with salt and pepper. Place the meat in the frying pan again and put the sauce on top. Cook at a moderate flame for 10 minutes.
