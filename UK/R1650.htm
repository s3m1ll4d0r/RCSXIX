   FOR 12 UNITS

1 kg of salted fish (soaked) - wreckfish, sea bass, corvina

1 onion

4 tomatoes

2 cloves of garlic

1/2 l of oil

4 eggs

Desalt the fish well, leaving it to soak for 24 hours and changing the water several times. Use 4 tablespoons of oil to fry the onion, garlic and tomatoes. Strain when done. Then mince the fish without the skin and bones. Mix the fish well with the fried ingredients and the eggs. Heat the rest of the oil and spoon the mixture into the frying pan and fry the cakes on both sides. Place on absorbent paper when done.
