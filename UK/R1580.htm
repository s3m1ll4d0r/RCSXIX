"Puchero" Canarian Stew

   FOR 8 SERVINGS

1/2 kg of chick-peas

1 kg of veal

1 kg of salted ribs

1/2 kg of lean bacon

400 g of pumpkin

800 g of cabbage

300 g of green beans

500 g of marrow

250 g of potatoes

200 g of "chorizo" sausage (Spanish variety)

1 head of garlic

1 heaping tablespoon of paprika

1 level tablespoon of cumin

Cut 150 g of bacon into thin strips and set aside. Cook the rest of the bacon with the meat and chick-peas in 1 1/2 l of water. Place the potatoes and vegetables in a large pan, washed and cut in big pieces, along with the green beans tied in bunches. Cut the chorizo sausage into pieces and evenly distribute over the vegetables. Put the cooked meat and chick-peas on top. Make a puree with the cooked bacon, garlic, cumin, saffron, paprika and 500 cc of meat broth. Cover the vegetables with this. Place the strips of bacon on top and cover the pan well. Cook at a low flame, until the vegetables are tender.
