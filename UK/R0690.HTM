Egg Tortillas

Previous

Index 

Next

 

 

 

   FOR 4 SERVINGS

4 eggs

2 slices of sandwich bread

1 tomato

1 onion

100 cc of white wine

100 cc of oil

Parsley

Cinnamon

Clove

Aniseed

Black pepper

Mint

Thyme

Salt

Sugar

Soak the bread with the wine. Finely chop the onion, the tomato and the herbs. Crush the spices. Mix everything with the bread (torn into pieces) and the beaten eggs. Heat a tablespoon of oil in a small frying pan. Form the "tortillas" with a mixing spoon. Place on absorbent paper. To save time, you can use two frying pans at the same time.
