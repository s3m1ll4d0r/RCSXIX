Carrot Soup

   FOR 6 SERVINGS

1 kg of carrots

1 medium-sized onion

100 g of flour

100 g of butter

2 tablespoons of oil

1 tablespoon of sugar

2 tablespoons of fruit vinegar

1 bouillon cube

Peel the carrots and boil in one litre of water with the bouillon cube. Meanwhile, finely chop the onion. When the carrots are tender, remove them from the water and drain them without throwing away the water, and cut them into thin round slices. Brown the onion in the oil and add the carrot slices. In another pan melt the butter in the oil and cook the flour in it. Add the water that the carrots were boiled in and beat with a beater until there are no lumps. Combine with the vegetables and add the sugar and vinegar. Boil for 3 or 4 minutes.
