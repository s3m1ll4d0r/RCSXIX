   FOR 6 SERVINGS

500 g of puff pastry dough (home-made or frozen)

200 g of ground veal

200 g of ground pork

100 g of lean bacon

6 cloves of garlic

125 cc of white wine

Salt

Orange leaf

Thyme

Black pepper

Nutmeg

Clove

Spread the puff pastry dough out and cut into 6 equal parts, giving them the shape you want. Mark circles in the center of each piece. Bake in the oven at a medium temperature for 20 minutes. Meanwhile, prepare the filling. Mince the bacon very fine and cook. When all the fat has come out add the meat, the ground garlic, the ground spices, the thyme and the whole orange leaf. Add the wine and stir with a wooden spoon until all is well cooked. Take the timbal crusts out of the oven, removing the tops carefully. Fill with the meat and replace the tops. Bake in the oven for a few minutes at a very high temperature.
