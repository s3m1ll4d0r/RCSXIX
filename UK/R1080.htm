Salted Wreckfish Casserole (or use Fresh Fish)

   FOR 8 SERVINGS

1 kg of salted wreckfish

2 kg of peeled potatoes

1 onion

1 tomato

6 cloves of garlic

1 tablespoon of cooking fat

100 cc of oil

1 kg of gofio (toasted wheat flour)

Parsley

Saffron

Clove

For the "mojo" sauce:

1 head of garlic

1 tablespoon of cumin

1 tablespoon of saffron

2 tablespoons of paprika

1 red hot pepper

100 cc of vinegar

250 cc of oil

Salt

Chop the onion, the garlic, the tomato and the parsley. Fry it in the oil until the onion is translucent. Put the potatoes and the fish on top, with the skin up. Put enough water in the pan to cover the potatoes and put a lid on it. Bring to a boil and add 3 or 4 cloves crushed with the saffron and mixed with the cooking fat. Meanwhile, prepare the "mojo" sauce, first by crushing the peeled garlic with the salt and pepper and then adding the rest of the ingredients. When the potatoes are cooked, remove the fish and put a few tablespoons of "mojo" sauce on top. Mix the gofio into the broth and serve on the side.
