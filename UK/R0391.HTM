   FOR 4 SERVINGS

1 kg of marrow

1/4 l of tomato sauce

200 g of grated cheese

Cut the marrow into round slices and boil in salted water. When they are tender, but not overly done, remove and drain. Add the tomato sauce and simmer over a low flame. Serve with the grated cheese sprinkled on top.

Per Serving

Protein (G) 7 Carbohydrates (G) 25
Fats (G) 19 Fibre (G) 2
