   FOR 4 SERVINGS

1 kg of mackerel

1 onion

4-5 cloves of garlic

125 cc of oil

62.5 cc of vinegar

Black pepper

Oregano

Thyme

Salt

Clean the mackerel, cut in round slices and add salt. Chop the onion and garlic and place in a pan. Add the oil, vinegar and 250 cc of water. Boil until the onion is tender. Add the spices. Steam the fish on a grate that can be placed inside the pan (or in a special steamer). Cook for 4 minutes, covered. Turn the slices of fish and cook for another 3 minutes. Serve with the sauce separately.
