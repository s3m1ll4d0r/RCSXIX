Coconut Sponge Cake

   FOR 6 SERVINGS

250 g of confectioners' sugar

250 g of grated coconut

4 eggs

4 slices of bread

125 cc of milk

Butter to grease the mold

Preheat the oven. Grease the mold with butter, placing the parchment paper, also greased, on the bottom. Cut off the crust from the bread and sprinkle with milk. Beat the egg yolks and mix with the coconut and the sugar. Beat the egg whites till stiff. Break up the bread with a fork and combine with the coconut mix. Add the egg whites, mixing carefully. Pour into the mold. Bake for 45 minutes at a moderate temperature.
