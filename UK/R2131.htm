"Huevos Moles" (dessert of egg yolks and sugar)

Index

   FOR 8 SERVINGS

250 g of sugar.

12 egg yolks.

4 bitter almonds.

50 g of roasted crushed almonds.

Make a syrup to a thread consistency using the sugar and 250 cc of water. Allow to cool. Crush the almonds and soak in two spoonfuls of hot water. Beat the yolks till white and frothy. Strain the liquid from the almonds, mix with the yolks and slowly pour, while stirring, over the cool syrup. Double boil while beating with a whisk until thick. Remove from the flame and continue stirring for at least two hours if by hand, or 20-30 minutes with an electric beater, until cool. Serve in small dessert cups and top with the roasted almonds.
