   FOR 6 SERVINGS

1 kg of beef loin (in one piece)

200 g of lean bacon

200 g of salchichon (similar to salami)

2 onions

2 tomatoes

250 cc of white wine

100 cc of oil

1 teaspoon of paprika

Clove

Nutmeg

Thyme

Cut the bacon in strips, and coat with the paprika mixed with the crushed clove and the grated nutmeg. Lard the strips of bacon into the meat, lengthwise. Heat the oil and brown the meat on all sides. Add the onions cut in thick pieces and cover. When the onions are tender, add the peeled and seeded tomatoes, the thyme and the wine. Cook at a low flame, well covered for one hour, turning the meat every 15 minutes. If it needs more liquid add hot water with a little salt. Take out the meat and cut into round slices. Garnish with slices of salchichon (or salami). Strain the sauce and serve on the side.
