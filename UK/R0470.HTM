   FOR 4 SERVINGS

1 kg of onions

4 cloves of garlic

2 laurel leaves

100 of oil

1 bouillon cube

1 teaspoon of sugar

125 cc of white wine

Leave the onions whole if they are small. If not, cut them into pieces. Heat the oil with the minced garlic and laurel leaves added. Add the onions and fry. Add the wine, the bouillon cube and the sugar. Cover and cook at a low flame for 20 minutes, moving the pan occasionally.
