Chicken Cooked in Its Own Broth

Index

   FOR 4 SERVINGS

1 chicken

1 kg of onions

2 lemons

500 cc of the chicken stock

50 g of flour

100 cc of oil

Parsley

Salt

After boiling the chicken, take it out of its broth and cut it into pieces. Fry the onions in the oil along with the finely chopped parsley. Add the chicken and cover. Cook at a low flame for 10 minutes. Dissolve a tablespoon of flour in 500 cc of broth. Add the chicken and cook for a half an hour, with the flame very low. Place the chicken on a hot tray. Add the juice of two lemons to the sauce, bring to a boil and pour over the chicken.
