Red Mullet in Sauce

   FOR 6 SERVINGS

6 large red mullets

1 head of garlic

2 tablespoons of flour

125 cc of oil

2 tablespoons of breadcrumbs

1 onion

2 lemons

Parsley

Black pepper

1 small can of anchovies

Salt

Clean the fish and keep the roe and the livers for later. Coat the fish with flour. Peel the garlic and crush along with the parsley and salt. Combine with half of the oil and the lemon juice. Coat the fish with this mixture and bake. At the same time cook the roe and the livers. Meanwhile, mince the onion and fry in the rest of the oil. Crush the livers with the anchovies, mix with the fried onion, the lemon juice and the rest of the crushed garlic. Simmer for a few minutes and serve with the red mullet.
