   FOR 30 SERVINGS

250 g of flour

100 g of sugar

50 g of ground almonds

50 g of chopped almonds

2 eggs

Grated lemon peel

Beat the eggs whites and the yolks together, with the sugar and the grated lemon peel added. Mix with the two types of almonds and the flour. Preheat the oven. Sprinkle the baking sheets with flour. Spread the dough out over a floured surface. Cut in rectangles. Place on the baking sheets. Bake for 30 minutes at a moderate temperature.
