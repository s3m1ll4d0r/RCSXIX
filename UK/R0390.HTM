Cauliflower Soup

   FOR 4 SERVINGS

600 g of cauliflower

1 tomato

1 onion

1 litre of chicken stock (you can use cubes)

2 tablespoons of oil

Break the branches of the cauliflower off from the hard center stock. Boil them until they are tender. Drain. Chop the onion and fry in the oil. Add the peeled, chopped tomato, the cooked cauliflower and the chicken stock. Cook for 10 minutes and reduce to a puree.
