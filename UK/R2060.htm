   FOR 20 UNITS

8 egg yolks

250 g of sugar

Cinnamon

Lemon peel

Oil for the molds

Grease 6 molds with oil. Preheat the oven. Beat the yolks until they are very frothy. Place the cream in the molds. Cover with aluminum foil and double boil in the oven for 25 minutes at a moderate temperature. Meanwhile, using sugar and 250 cc of water, make a syrup to a thread consistency, adding cinnamon and lemon peel. Remove the yolks from the molds when they cool, and prick them with a pin in several places. Pour the syrup, not very hot, over them. Serve cold.
