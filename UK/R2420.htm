Roscones (A type of doughnut)

   FOR 6 SERVINGS

300 g of flour

300 g of sugar

2 eggs

8 tablespoons of milk

1 tablespoon of butter

1 tablespoon of aniseed

1/2 tablespoon of cinnamon powder

Grated lemon peel

2 eggs

1 l of oil (for frying)

Boil the milk with the butter, the aniseed, the cinnamon and the grated lemon peel. Cover and let stand. Make a syrup with 200 g of sugar and 250 cc of water. When the milk cools strain and combine with the flour. Add the eggs and mix without beating. Add 100 g of sugar. Heat the oil. Form doughnuts and fry in very hot oil. Place on absorbent paper. Place in the hot syrup. Boil for a minute, making sure to cover completely. Place on a baking sheet and dry for a few minutes in the oven.
