   FOR 20 SERVINGS

300 g of grated coconut

300 g of sugar

Make a thick syrup. Take half and add the coconut to it. Heat over a moderate flame, constantly stirring. Spread out over a marble surface and when sufficiently cool work it with your hands until smooth. Make small balls, place on a fork and dip in the remaining syrup. Place on a greased plate until cool.
