   FOR 6 SERVINGS

200 g of ground almonds

200 g of sugar

4 eggs

150 cc of port wine

Ground cinnamon

Preheat the oven and prepare the mold by greasing it with butter and flouring it. Beat the egg yolks. Mix with the sugar, the port wine, the almonds and the cinnamon. Now beat the egg whites till stiff and mix with the rest of the ingredients. Place in a mold and bake for 30 minutes at a moderate temperature.
