Scrambled Eggs in a Nest

   FOR 6 SERVINGS

6 eggs

50 g of ham

200 g of peeled and seeded tomatoes

200 g of cooked peas

1 small onion

1/2 l of oil

12 slices of bread

Salt Parsley

Cut a square out of the centre of 6 slices of bread. Heat the oil, fry the bread and place the slices on absorbent paper. Chop the onion and fry it in two tablespoons of oil in a thick frying pan. When the onion is translucent add the tomatoes. When the tomatoes are done add the chopped ham and the peas. Beat the eggs, adding salt, and mix with the above ingredients, cooking at a low flame. Place each slice of bread with the square cut out on top of each whole slice and fill with the egg mixture. Garnish with the squares that have been cut out and the chopped parsley.
