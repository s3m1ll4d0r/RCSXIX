   FOR 1.5 KG (APPROX.)

1 kg of orange peels

1 kg of sugar

Cut the peels into large pieces. Boil until tender. Drain and weigh. For every kg of peels make a syrup with 1 kg of sugar and 500 cc of water. When the syrup obtains a thread consistency add the peels. Let stand for 24 hours. The following day remove the peels from the syrup and boil it until it is very thick. Remove from the flame and mix again with the peels. Let stand another 24 hours. Repeat the same operation for 4 days. The syrup will become thicker and thicker. On the fifth day remove the peels, place on a rack and leave them in the oven at a very low temperature for 5 hours, to dry them. Keep in well sealed containers.
