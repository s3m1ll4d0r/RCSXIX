   FOR 8 SERVINGS

300 g of minced pork

200 g of ground almonds

100 g of sugar

2 eggs

500 g of puff pastry

Cinnamon

Nutmeg

Flatten out the puff pastry dough and line a pie pan with it, reserving a piece of the dough to cover it. Preheat the oven. Mix the meat with the almonds, the sugar, the ground spices and two lightly beaten eggs. Use this to fill the pie pan and cover with the rest of the dough, making a small hole in it so that the vapor can escape. Beat the remaining egg and use it to coat the top. Bake for 45 minutes at a medium temperature. Serve hot or cold.
