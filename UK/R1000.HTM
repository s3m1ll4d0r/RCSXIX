   FOR 4 SERVINGS

1 kg of squid

1 onion

6 cloves of garlic

2 hard-boiled eggs

125 cc of oil

1 lemon

Parsley

Paprika

Ground black pepper

Salt

Wash the squid and cut into rings. Cook them with 250 cc of water and little salt. Meanwhile, heat the oil. Fry the onion and the garlic, finely chopped. Add the chopped parsley, the hard-boiled eggs, also chopped, the paprika and the pepper. Add all the squid, together with the lemon juice. Boil at a low flame until the squid is tender.
