Spanish Tortillas with Meat or Fish

   FOR 4 SERVINGS

250 g of meat or fish (leftovers)

5 eggs

2 tablespoons of flour

2 teaspoons of cinnamon

1 teaspoon of aniseed

2 tablespoons of parsley

1 tomato

1 onion

200 g of sugar

1/2 l of oil

Make a syrup with the sugar (reserving one teaspoon for later), 250 cc of water and a teaspoon of cinnamon. Break the fish or meat into pieces. Peel, seed and chop the tomato. Add to the syrup. Add the finely minced onion, the parsley, the aniseed, the tablespoon of sugar and the rest of the cinnamon. Beat the eggs, together with the flour, without separating the white from the yolk. Thoroughly mix everything together. Heat the oil and put tablespoons of the mixture in it, frying the Spanish tortillas on each side. Remove and place on absorbent paper. Pour the syrup over the top.
