Almond Sponge Cake

   FOR 8 SERVINGS

200 g of ground almonds.

200 g of sugar.

2 eggs.

1 egg yolk.

Butter or margarine for the mold.

Topping: 200 g of ground sugar.

1 lemon.

1/2 egg white.

Preheat the oven. Mix the almonds with the sugar. Combine the eggs, whites and yolks together, without beating. When the mixture is smooth place in the mold and bake for 15-20 minutes. In the meantime, prepare the topping. Lightly beat the egg white. Mix the sugar with the lemon juice and add the egg white in small spoonfuls. Remove the cake from the oven and, while still hot, paint the topping onto it with a brush.
