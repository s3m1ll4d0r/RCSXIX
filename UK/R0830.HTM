Fish Marinade

   FOR 4 SERVINGS

1 kg of fish (e.g. conger eel)

2 oranges

50 g of raisins

50 g of almonds

250 cc of vinegar

250 cc of white wine

250 cc of oil

Salt

Clean the fish, sprinkle with salt and fry in very hot oil. Place the fish in a deep baking pan. Strain the oil that remains after frying the fish and mix with the vinegar, the wine and the orange juice. Crush the garlic along with half an orange peel. Combine with the sauce, the almonds and the raisins. Boil for 5 minutes. Pour over the fish. Serve cold.
