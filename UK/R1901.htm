   FOR 4 SERVINGS

6 bananas.

300 g of sugar.

4 eggs.

2 egg yolks.

1 lemon.

Cinnamon powder.

Cut the bananas into slices and mix with the cinnamon, the grated lemon and 250 g. of sugar. Cover with plastic and leave in the refrigerator for at least eight hours. Blend with the lemon juice, and add the whole eggs and the yolks. With the remaining sugar and a tablespoonful of water, make the caramel in the mold. Place the mixture in the mold and double boil for 30 minutes at a low flame.
