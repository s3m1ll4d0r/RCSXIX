Cabbage and Chick-Pea Soup

Index

   FOR 6 SERVINGS

250 g of chick-peas (soaked beforehand)

800 g of red and white cabbage

2 l of broth

100 cc of oil

2 cloves of garlic

Cut the cabbage in thin strips and brown in the oil. Cover and cook at a low flame until it softens in its own juice. Crush and add the garlic. Meanwhile, cook the chick-peas in the broth. When they are tender add the cabbage and cook for 3 or 4 minutes.
