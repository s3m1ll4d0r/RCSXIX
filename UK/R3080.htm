   FOR 1 KG

1 kg of white scallions

1 l of vinegar

1 tablespoon of salt

1 teaspoon of black pepper

1 hot pepper

6 cloves

6 cloves of garlic

1 small piece of ginger root (or 1 teaspoon if in powder form)

1 teaspoon of thyme

1 teaspoon of oregano

2 laurel leaves

Peel the onions and let stand in salt water. Boil the vinegar with the rest of the ingredients. When the vinegar cools drain the water from the onions and put them in jars. Pour the vinegar with the spices into the jars. Close well. They're ready to eat in a week's time.
