Glossary

Start



The purpose of this glossary is to clarify the meaning of many of the words found in the recipes that we have worked with whose use are uncommon, archaic or colloquial. When it has been possible we have indicated its accepted Spanish meaning (SP). In other cases we have resorted to the usage of CRISTOBAL CORRALES and company, in "Tesoro lexicográfico del español de Canarias", published by the Real Academia Española and the Canarian Government, 1992 (CO), and in others to the interpretation of ALEJANDRO CIORNESCU (CI) or our own (AU).
