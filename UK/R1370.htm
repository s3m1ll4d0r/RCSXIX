Steaks with Walnut and Almond Sauce

   FOR 4 SERVINGS

1 kg of veal steaks

1 onion

2 cloves of garlic

1 tomato

100 g of walnuts

100 g of almonds

50 g of stale or oven-hardened bread

100 g of bacon

100 g of raisins

125 cc of white wine

125 cc of oil

125 cc of beef stock

Parsley

Saffron

Black pepper

Clove

Fry the steaks in the oil and set aside. Fry the onion, the tomato and the garlic in the same oil in which the meat was fried. Add the minced bacon, the almonds, the walnuts and the bread. Turn the steaks and add the wine, the beef stock and the spices. Strain and pour over the meat. Add the raisins and cook at a moderate flame for 10 minutes.
