Almond Cake

   FOR 6 SERVINGS

225 g of ground almonds.

150 g of sugar.

50 g of butter.

50 g of flour.

4 lumps of sugar.

1 lemon.

2 eggs.

150 cc of sweet wine

Rub the lumps of sugar with the lemon peel until they are well saturated. Melt in a saucepan with the lemon juice, the lemon peel, cut into very fine strips, and the wine until the mixture is reduced to half its original volume. Preheat the oven. Grease the mold with the butter. Beat the butter and the sugar until creamy. Add the yolks one by one. Beat the whites till stiff. Mix everything with the ground almonds and the sifted flour. Add some wine and mix. Pour the mixture into the mold and bake for 20 minutes at a medium temperature.
