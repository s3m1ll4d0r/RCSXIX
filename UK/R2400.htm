   FOR 6 SERVINGS

4 egg whites

15 sponge fingers

3 lemons

200 g of confectioners' sugar

4 sheets of gelatin

Cinnamon powder

Oil for greasing the mold

Grease a mold with oil. Place the sponge fingers vertically against the sides. Cut them if they extend higher than the top. Squeeze the lemon juice and mix with the gelatin. Beat the egg whites till stiff and add the sugar and a little cinnamon. Mix with the semi-done gelatin, beating continuously. Fill the mold with the mixture. Place in the refrigerator for at least 2 hours. Place in hot water for a few seconds in order to take it cleanly out of the mold.
