Chick-Pea Soup

   FOR 4 SERVINGS

1/2 kg of chick-peas

2 hard-boiled eggs

125 cc of tomato puree

50 g of almonds

100 cc of oil

2 beef bouillon cubes

4 cloves of garlic

Parsley

Boil the chick-peas with the bouillon cubes in 1 1/2 litres of water. Fry the almonds with the garlic and the parsley. Reduce to a puree, together with the hard-boiled eggs and the tomato puree. Combine the chick-peas when they are tender. Boil everything together for a few minutes.
