Stew with Boiled Meat

   FOR 4 SERVINGS

300 g of boiled meat

1 tomato

1 onion

4 or 5 cloves of garlic

1/2 kg of potatoes

250 cc of oil

1/2 teaspoon of ground black pepper

1 teaspoon of salt

1 teaspoon of thyme

2 cloves

a pinch of sugar

Cut the potatoes into cubes, fry and set aside for later. Cut the meat into pieces, brown lightly and set aside as well. Cut the onion in thin strips, the garlic in round slices and brown in the same oil with the peeled and seeded tomato. When the tomato is partially reduced, add the meat, the spices and 375 cc of water. Cook at a low flame. When it's about half done add the potatoes.
