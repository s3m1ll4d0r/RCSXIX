Roasted Veal

   FOR 6 SERVINGS

1 kg of sirloin or veal back (in one piece)

125 cc of beef stock

125 cc of white wine

1 tablespoon of cooking fat

1 teaspoon of salt

1 teaspoon of meat spices

1 teaspoon of flour

Dissolve the cooking fat in the hot beef stock. Mix with the wine, salt and spices. Cover the meat with the mixture and let stand for at least 2 hours. Heat the oven and cook the meat for 10 minutes at a high temperature. Reduce the temperature and cook for 30 minutes, basting occasionally. Place the meat on a hot tray. Put the sauce in a pan and heat while stirring in a teaspoon of flour and beating with a whisk. Serve with the sauce on the side.
