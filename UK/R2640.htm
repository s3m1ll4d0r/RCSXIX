   FOR 6 SERVINGS

250 cc of milk.

200 g sugar.

6 eggs.

Lemon peel.

Cinnamon.

Vanilla.

Boil the milk with 150 g of sugar, the lemon peel, the cinnamon and the vanilla. In the mold, make a caramel with the remaining 50 g. of sugar. Beat the eggs omelette style. Add the strained milk and mix with a wooden spoon. Pour the mixture into the mold. Place in the oven and double boil at a medium temperature for 30 minutes. Let cool in the mold.
