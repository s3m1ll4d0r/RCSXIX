Beef Stew

   FOR 6 SERVINGS

1/2 l of oil

1 kg of potatoes

1/2 kg of cooked meat (leftovers)

250 cc of beef stock

6 cloves of garlic

1 tablespoon of flour

Parsley

1 lemon

Cut the potatoes into cubes and fry them in the hot oil. Remove and set aside. Cut the meat into pieces and fry as well. Take four tablespoons of the oil, mix with the flour and cook lightly. Crush the garlic with the parsley and mix with the flour. Add the beef stock, mixing with a whisk. When it thickens a little add the potatoes and the meat. Boil for a few seconds and add the lemon juice.
