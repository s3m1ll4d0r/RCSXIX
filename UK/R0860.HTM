Rabbit in "Mojo" Sauce

   FOR 4 SERVINGS

1 rabbit

200 g of oil

250 cc of broth

125 cc of wine

7-8 cloves of garlic

Red chili pepper

Cumin

Oregano

Thyme

Clean and cut the rabbit into pieces. Roast in the oven at a high temperature for 10 minutes. Remove from the oven and fry in oil. Crush the liver along with the garlic, the spices, the broth and the wine. Pour this over the top of the rabbit and cook at a low flame for 35 minutes.
