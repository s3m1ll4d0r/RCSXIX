   FOR 4 SERVINGS

1 hen (approx. 1,200 g)

100 g of ground almonds

100 g of butter

250 cc of wine

1/4 teaspoon of sugar

1 teaspoon of salt

4 or 5 cloves of garlic

6 black peppercorns

2 cloves

A few strands of saffron

1/2 teaspoon of cinnamon

Clean the chicken and place it in a pan. Add all the ingredients. Cover well and cook at a very low flame for one hour turning it every 15 minutes.

Per Serving

Protein (G) 65 Carbohydrates (G) 5
Fats (G) 67 Fibre (G) 1
Cholesterol (MG) 353 Calories (KCal) 890
