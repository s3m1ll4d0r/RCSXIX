Pork Liver Roast

   FOR 4 SERVINGS

500 g of pork liver

1 onion

6 cloves of garlic

50 g of corinth raisins

50 g of chopped almonds

100 cc of oil

250 cc of broth

125 cc of white wine

Cinnamon powder

Thyme

Laurel

Cut the pork liver into small strips. Cut the onion into thin strips and brown together with the garlic in the hot oil. When the onions begin to get translucent add the raisins and the almonds. Add the meat and the spices, mixing well. Stir for a couple of minutes and add the wine and the broth. Cover and cook for 15 minutes at a low flame.
