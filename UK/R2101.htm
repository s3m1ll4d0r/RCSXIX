   FOR 20 UNITS

12 egg yolks.

500 g of sugar.

Grated lemon peel.

Mix the yolks with half the sugar and the grated lemon peel. Place on a very low flame while stirring constantly with a wooden spoon until the mixture thickens. Spread the paste on a tray, adding more sugar if necessary. Give shape to the balls when sufficiently cool to do so. Make a syrup to a soft ball consistency using the rest of the sugar and half a glass of water. Pick up each ball with a fork and place in the syrup, which will have been removed from the flame. Place on a greased grid until cool.
