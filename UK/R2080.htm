   FOR 6 SERVINGS

180 g of flour.

120 g of sugar.

120 g of butter.

100 g of candied fruit or raisins.

1 teaspoon of baking powder.

3 eggs.

Preheat the oven, grease the mold with butter and flour, and place greased parchment paper on the bottom of the mold. Beat the butter with the sugar until the cream is white and frothy. Add the yolks and continue beating. Sift the flour together with the baking powder. Beat the whites until stiff. Mix the fruit with a spoonful of flour. Mix the cream with the flour. Add the fruit and mix everything with the whites. Place in the mold and bake for 35 minutes at a medium temperature.
