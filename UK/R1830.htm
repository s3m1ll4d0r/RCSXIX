   FOR 6 SERVINGS

6 apples

3 eggs

50 g of sugar

Cinnamon

Grated lemon peel

Butter (to grease the mold)

Peel the apples and cut them into pieces. Cook them in 2 or 3 tablespoons of water, together with the sugar, cinnamon and grated lemon peel. When the apples are soft, reduce them to a puree. Beat the eggs well and mix them with the apple puree. Grease the mold with butter, pour in the mixture and cover. Double boil for one hour over a moderate flame.
