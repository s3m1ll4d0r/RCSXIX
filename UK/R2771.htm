   FOR 6 SERVINGS

200 g of frangollo flour

2 egg yolks.

100 g of almonds.

100 g of raisins.

150 g of sugar.

Cinnamon Powder

Grated lemon peel.

Aniseed

Honey

Mix the flour with the milk, the sugar and the butter. Add the raisins, the almonds, the cinnamon, the lemon peel and the aniseed. Place on the flame and stir with a wooden spoon. Beat the yolks, add to the mixture and cook until thick. Leave to cool and serve with honey.
