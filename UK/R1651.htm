Salted Wreckfish Stew

   FOR 4 SERVINGS

500 g of potatoes

1 kg of salted wreckfish

1 onion

2 tomatoes

1/2 l of oil (for frying)

250 cc of fish stock (you can use cubes) ???

2 tablespoons of vinegar

2 cloves of garlic

Parsley

Desalt the fish well, soaking for at least 8 hours. Cut into pieces. Cut the potatoes into round slices and fry in the oil. Cut the onions and the peeled tomatoes into round slices as well. Place everything in a casserole dish, starting with the onion, the fish, the tomatoes and the fried potatoes. Add the parsley and the minced garlic. Pour the fish stock, the vinegar and 2 tablespoons of oil over the top. Cover and cook for 20 minutes at a moderate flame.
