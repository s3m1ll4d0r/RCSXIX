   FOR 6 SERVINGS

1 l of milk

4 egg yolks

100 g of butter

500 g of sugar

Vanilla powder

Boil the milk with the butter. Remove from the flame, add the sugar and the vanilla and mix well. When it cools, beat the yolks until frothy and slowly add the milk. If you do not have an ice cream maker, place in a baking pan and place in the freezer. Take it out every 20 minutes to beat with a fork in order to break up the ice crystals that form. It will be ready in about 4 hours. Take out of the freezer a few minutes before serving.
