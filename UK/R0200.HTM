Creamed Chard or Spinach

   FOR 4 SERVINGS

1 bunch of chard

1 tablespoon of flour

250 cc of beef stock

250 cc of milk

1 tablespoon of butter

Cut the chard into fine strips. Cover and boil with the beef stock. At the same time cook the flour in the butter. Add the milk and beat with a whisk. When the chard is tender add the sauce and boil for 2 or 3 minutes.
