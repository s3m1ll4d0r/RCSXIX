Fresh Roasted Sardines

   FOR 4 SERVINGS

1 kg of small sardines

250 cc of oil

1 onion

6 cloves of garlic

1 small hot pepper

4 tablespoons of vinegar

Black peppercorns

Clean the sardines and remove the heads. Place on a grid over a baking pan and roast the sardines at a high temperature. Meanwhile, finely mince the onion as well as the garlic. Crush the black pepper. Mix with the oil and the vinegar. Boil at a moderate flame for 7-8 minutes. Take the sardines out of the oven, place on a tray and pour the sauce over them. Serve hot or cold.
