Mackerel Casserole

   FOR 6 SERVINGS

6 mackerels

1 onion

50 g of raisins

1 lemon

1/2 l of oil

250 cc of fish stock

6 cloves of garlic

Ground black pepper

Saffron

Salt

Clean the fish, dry well and sprinkle with salt. Heat the oil and fry the fish. Place the fish in a baking pan. Strain the oil. Mince the onion and the garlic and fry with 4 tablespoons of the strained oil. When the onion is translucent, add a little pepper, the raisins, the crushed saffron, the fish stock and the lemon juice. Cook for 4 or 5 minutes. Pour the sauce over the fish and bake in a hot oven for 5 minutes.
