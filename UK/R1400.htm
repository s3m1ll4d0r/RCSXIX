Steak with Almonds

Index

   FOR 4 SERVINGS

1 kg of beef steaks

4 or 5 cloves of garlic

100 g of almonds

250 cc of beef stock (you can use cubes)

125 cc of oil

1/2 teaspoon of ground black pepper

1 tablespoon of chopped parsley

Fry the garlic and the almonds in the oil. Remove and set aside half of the almonds. Crush the remaining almonds with the garlic and add to the beef stock. Fry the steaks in the same oil. Place them in a pan and add the sauce. Cook at a low flame, adding the parsley and the pepper. Garnish with the remaining fried almonds.
