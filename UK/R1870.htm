Rosquetes Milk-Bread Style

   FOR 4 SERVINGS

250 g of fresh cheese.

200 g of flour.

150 g of sugar.

1 egg

Grated lemon peel

1/2 teaspoonful of cinnamon powder.

½ litre of cooking oil

Make a syrup with the sugar and 250 cc of water. Beat the cheese and the egg. Add the lemon peel and the cinnamon. Mix with the flour. Heat the oil. Give shape to the rosquetes and fry in the oil. As you take them out, place in the syrup and boil for 2 or 3 minutes, making sure they are completely covered with the syrup. Place on a baking sheet and dry in the oven for a few minutes.
