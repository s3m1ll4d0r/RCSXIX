Vegetable Pancake

   FOR 4 SERVINGS

1 bunch of chard or spinach

1 onion

4 eggs

3 tablespoons of oil

2 tablespoons of bread crumbs

Salt

Wash the chard or spinach and cut into very thin strips. Chop the onion and fry with 2 tablespoons of oil. When the onions become translucent add the chard or the spinach and a little salt. Cover and let cook at a low flame until the vegetable is tender. Meanwhile, beat the eggs, add salt and mix with the bread crumbs. Add the vegetable. Heat the remaining oil in a frying pan and fry the pancake on both sides. If you use frozen spinach it should be completely thawed and drained before using. Mix with the onion when it is completely cooked, and only leave it over the flame for 2 to 3 minutes.
