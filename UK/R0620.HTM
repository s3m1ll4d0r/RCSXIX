   FOR 4 SERVINGS

1 kg of turnips

1 onion

250 cc of beef stock (you can use cubes)

100 cc of vinegar

Thyme

Parsley

1/2 l of cooking oil (there will be some left over)

Peel the turnips and cut them into round slices about a half centimeter thick. Heat the oil and fry the turnips. Mince the onion very fine and brown it in 2 tablespoons of oil. Add the fried turnips together with the vinegar, the beef stock, the parsley and the thyme. Cover and cook at a very low flame until the turnips are tender (approx. 20 minutes).
