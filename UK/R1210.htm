Meatballs

   FOR 4 SERVINGS

500 g of ground beef

1 tomato

2 slices of bread without the crust

125 cc of oil

250 cc of broth

Mint

Clove

Saffron

2 eggs

Salt

Beat the peeled and seeded tomato, the bread and the spices. Take half of the mixture and add the meat and the egg yolks. Make the meatballs. Lightly beat the egg whites and dip the meatballs in them. Heat the oil and fry. Mix the rest of the ingredients with the beef stock. Place the meatballs in the sauce and cook for a few minutes.
