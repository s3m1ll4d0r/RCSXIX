Chicken Fricassee

   FOR 4 SERVINGS

1 chicken

2 slices of bread

8 cloves of garlic

50 g of almonds

250 cc of chicken stock (you can use cubes)

100 cc of oil

250 cc of white wine

Saffron

After boiling the chicken and removing the bones, cut it into pieces (not too small). Fry the garlic, the almonds, the bread and the saffron all together. Mix it all together with a beater, along with the stock and the wine. Pour over the chicken and cook for a few minutes at a low flame, until the sauce thickens.
