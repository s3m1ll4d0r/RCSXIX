Cod Stew

   FOR 4 SERVINGS

300 g of cod

50 g of flour

1 onion

2 hard-boiled eggs

125 cc of oil

Parsley

Boil the cod after soaking. Drain and keep the water. Cut the fish into pieces, removing the skin and bones. Heat the oil. Coat the cod with flour and fry. Remove, and in the same oil fry the minced onion. Add the cod and 250 cc of the water the cod was boiled in. Simmer for a few minutes, until the sauce thickens. Garnish with parsley and the chopped hard-boiled eggs.
