   FOR APPROXIMATELY 800 c.c.

1 kg of apricots without the pit.

1,2 kg of sugar.

Peel the apricots, cut them in half and remove the pits. For 1 kg. of fruit make a syrup to a soft ball consistency with 1.2 kg. of sugar and 500 cc of water. Add the apricots and cook on a low flame until tender but whole. Remove the apricots with care and place in jars. Heat the syrup and bring it again to a soft ball consistency. Pour the syrup over the fruit and leave to cool before covering.
