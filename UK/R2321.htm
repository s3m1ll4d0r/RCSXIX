   FOR 25 UNITS

100 g ground almonds

100 g of crushed almonds (chunky)

1 kg of confectioners' sugar

3 egg whites

Oil to grease the baking sheets

Lightly beat the egg whites. Mix the almonds with the sugar. Spoon in the egg whites until you have a paste that can be worked with. Preheat the oven. Prepare the baking sheets with parchment paper that has been greased with oil. Make small balls with the mix and place on the baking sheets, keeping them separated. Press down lightly. Place in the oven preheated to a moderate temperature for 35 minutes. Let them cool on the baking sheets and then remove.
