   FOR 6 SERVINGS

6 large onions

100 g of ground beef

125 cc of white wine

2 tablespoons of oil

250 cc of broth

2 egg yolks

Saffron

Mint

Peel the onions and hollow out the center with a sharp knife. Finely mince the part of the onion taken out of the center and fry it in the oil along with the chopped mint. When the onion is tender add the meat and a tablespoon of wine. Remove from the flame and add the egg yolks. Use this to fill the onions. In a pan in which the onions fit loosely, boil the wine with the stock for 5 minutes. Place the onions in the pan, cover and cook at a very low flame for 35 minutes.
