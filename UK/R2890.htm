   FOR APPROXIMATELY 800 cc

1 kg of peeled pears without the core.

1 kg of sugar.

Peel and remove the cores, and weigh the pears. For 1 kg. of fruit make a syrup to a thread consistency with 1 kg. of sugar and 500 cc of water. Cut the pears into pieces and add. Cook on a low flame, while stirring occasionally until the fruit is cooked and the marmalade thick.
