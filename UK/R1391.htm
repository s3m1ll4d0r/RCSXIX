Ropa Vieja (Old Clothes)

   FOR 4 SERVINGS

300 g of cooked meat

1/2 kg of potatoes

1/2 kg of cooked chick-peas

2 onions

250 cc of white wine

250 cc of stew broth

125 cc of oil

1 tablespoon of flour

Laurel

Black pepper

Cut the potatoes in thick round slices and fry them. Set aside for later when done. Cut the onion in thin strips. Fry the onions in the oil until transparent. Add the flour and stir well. Mix with the wine and the broth, stirring with a wooden spoon until it thickens a little. Add the meat, the fried potatoes and the cooked chick-peas. Boil everything together for a few minutes.
