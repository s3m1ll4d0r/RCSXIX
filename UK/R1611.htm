White Sauce for Fish

   FOR 500 cc

1/2 l of cream

1 tablespoon of flour

1 tablespoon of butter

A piece of lemon peel

1 cube of fish broth

Mix all of the ingredients with a beater. Simmer while stirring with a wooden spoon.
