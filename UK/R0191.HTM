   FOR 8 SERVINGS

1 kg of peas

250 cc of milk

3 egg yolks

1 tablespoon of butter

1 tablespoon of flour

Nutmeg.

Salt.

Boil the peas in salted water. At the same time bring the milk to a boil with the butter added. Add the flour and beat with a whisk. Beat the egg yolks with a little salt and grated nutmeg added. Remove from the flame and mix with the milk. When the peas are tender add them to the sauce. Heat the mixture without bringing to a boil.
