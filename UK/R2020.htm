   FOR 12 UNITS

500 g of sugar.

4 eggs whites.

50 g of confectioners' sugar.

Make a syrup with the sugar and 250 cc of water having a thread consistency. Beat the egg whites till stiff, and mix with the confectioners' sugar. Keep the syrup on a very low flame, and pour in large spoonfuls of the mixture, being careful not to mix them. With a spoon pour a little syrup on each meringue. Remove with a skimmer and place in bowls. Continue until the meringue finishes. Serve cold.
