Lentils with Chestnuts

Index 

Next

 

 

 

   FOR 6 SERVINGS

250 g of lentils

100 g of dried chestnuts

1 onion

100 cc of oil

3 cloves

Salt

Chop the chestnuts into large pieces. Boil with the lentils, which have been covered with cold water (approximately 1 litre). Mince the onion very fine and fry it in the oil. Add the mashed cloves and the salt. When the lentils and the chestnuts are tender, add them to the fried ingredients. Allow it all to boil lightly for a few minutes.
