Milk Pudding and Sponge Fingers

   FOR 10 UNITS

12 sponge fingers

125 cc of milk

200 g of sugar

50 g of raisins

6 eggs

1 teaspoon of cornstarch

75 cc of rum

Grated lemon peel

Cinnamon powder

Butter for the mold

Preheat the oven. Grease the mold with butter. Dissolve the cornstarch in the milk. Beat the egg whites and the yolks together. Add the sugar, lemon, cinnamon, raisins, milk and rum. Pour a little of the mix onto the bottom of the mold. Then place a layer of cakes and cover with the mix. Continue placing new layers in this way until the ingredients are used up. Place in the oven and double boil at a low temperature for 45 minutes.
