   FOR 1 kg (APPROX.)

335 g of sugar

480 g of flour

2 egg yolks

125 cc of milk

15 g of baking powder

Dissolve the baking powder in lukewarm water. Beat the egg yolks and add the sugar. Mix with the flour and the milk. Preheat the oven. Take the batter and form balls with your hands. Bake at a medium temperature for 30 minutes. Break the balls into small pieces and put back into the oven to dry for 1 hour at a low temperature. Remove from the oven and let cool. Crush very fine and keep in well sealed containers. Serve with milk.
