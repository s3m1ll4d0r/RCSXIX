Navy Bean Soup

   FOR 4 SERVINGS

250 g of navy beans (soaked)

2 hard-boiled eggs

50 g of ground almonds

2 cloves of garlic

100 cc of oil

2 beef bouillon cubes

2 slices of sandwich bread

Boil the beans, with the beef stock cubes, until they are tender. Heat the oil and fry the whole garlic cloves until brown. Remove the garlic and set aside. Cut the bread into small squares and fry them in the same oil. Remove and set aside. Crush the garlic, the almonds, the egg yolks and the parsley together. Mix with the oil that is left over and add everything to the beans. Serve with the fried bread and the chopped egg whites of the hard-boiled eggs.
