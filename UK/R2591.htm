   FOR 4 SERVINGS

100 g of unpopped popcorn

1 tablespoon of oil

150 g of sugar

Put the oil and the popcorn in a large pan in which the kernels can be spread out in one layer. Cover well and place over a low flame. When it begins to pop start shaking the pan, without taking the lid off. About 2-3 minutes later remove from the flame and take the lid off when you hear no more popping. Make a caramel syrup with the sugar and 4 tablespoons of water. When it achieves the right thickness, remove from the flame and add the popcorn. Mix with a wooden spoon. Spread over a damp board until cool. Cut in squares and serve.
