Salted Wreckfish Stew

Previous

Index 

Next

 

 

 

   FOR 4 SERVINGS

500 g of salted wreckfish

250 cc of white wine

2 onions

100 cc of oil

Orange peel

Thyme

Parsley

Ground black pepper

After soaking the fish, drain well and cut into small pieces. Heat the oil and fry the pieces of fish. When brown add the onions, cut in thin strips. Fry until the onions are translucent. Add the wine, parsley, thyme, pepper and orange peel, cut in thin strips. Cover and cook at a low flame for 25 minutes.
