Beef Over Fried Bread with Sauce

   FOR 4 SERVINGS

4 slices of bread

200 g of boiled beef

1 small onion

1 tomato

1 tablespoon of paprika

2 tablespoons of white wine

250 cc of oil

2 hard-boiled eggs

100 cc of milk

Salt

Parsley

Cut the slices of bread in half lengthwise and soak in the milk. Chop or grind the meat very fine. Fry the well minced onion and garlic in 2 tablespoons. Add the peeled and seeded tomato. When the tomato is well reduced, add the paprika and the white wine. Mix everything with the meat. Heat the rest of the oil and fry the slices of bread on both sides. Place on absorbent paper. Place the meat on the slices of bread. Break up the hard-boiled eggs with a fork and place on top of the meat. Garnish with very finely chopped parsley.
