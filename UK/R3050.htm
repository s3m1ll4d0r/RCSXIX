   FOR 800 cc (APPROX.)

1 kg of guava puree (see recipe)

1 kg of sugar

Peel the guavas, liquefy with a beater and strain. Weigh the puree and for every kg add another kg of sugar. Mix well, cover and place in the refrigerator for 24 hours. Stir constantly with a wooden spoon while heating over a flame. It is done When you can see the bottom while scraping with the spoon. Pour into molds lined with parchment paper. Cover with paper and keep in a dry place.
