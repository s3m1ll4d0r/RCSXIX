   FOR 6 SERVINGS

1 1/2 kg of lamb

200 g of lean bacon

1 head of garlic

1 bunch of parsley

1 lemon

250 cc of white wine

125 cc of oil

Salt

Preheat the oven. When it reaches a high temperature place the whole piece of lamb inside on a roasting pan and leave for 10 minutes. Meanwhile, fry the very finely chopped onion in the oil. Crush the garlic and the salt. Remove the bacon and add the garlic, wine and parsley. Pour the sauce over the lamb and put back in the oven to roast at a moderate temperature for 25 minutes on each side. About 5 minutes before it is done sprinkle the lemon juice over it.
